## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Wrangler Willys 392 is powered by a 6.4-liter HEMI V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, allowing for seamless shifting and optimal performance.

The vehicle features a robust suspension system, with a Dana 44 heavy-duty axle in the front and a Dana 44 axle in the rear.

The Wrangler Willys 392 also boasts a disconnecting front sway bar, which allows for improved articulation and flexibility on the trail.

The vehicle's approach and departure angles are 32.5 degrees and 29.6 degrees, respectively, making it capable of tackling even the most challenging off-road terrain.
