# Introduction to Jeep's 250th Anniversary Campaign

The year 2026 marks a significant milestone in American history - the 250th anniversary of the nation's founding. To commemorate this occasion, Jeep has partnered with a symbol of American strength and freedom: Captain America. This collaboration is set to inspire a new generation of adventurers and patriots, emphasizing the values of courage, resilience, and the pursuit of liberty.

## History and Context of the Partnership

The partnership between Jeep and Captain America is rooted in their shared values of freedom, adventure, and the unwavering commitment to protecting and serving the nation. Jeep, with its rich history of producing vehicles that embody the spirit of adventure and off-road capability, finds a natural ally in Captain America, a character who has become synonymous with bravery and selflessness. This collaboration is not just a marketing campaign but a celebration of the American spirit, aiming to inspire people to explore, to discover, and to stand up for what they believe in.
