## Technical Details of the Hemi V-8 Conversion

The Hemi V-8 engine is paired with a 8-speed automatic transmission and a four-wheel-drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and a rear axle with a 3.73 gear ratio.

The suspension has also been upgraded to handle the increased power and weight of the Hemi V-8 engine, with the addition of coilovers and a rear sway bar.
