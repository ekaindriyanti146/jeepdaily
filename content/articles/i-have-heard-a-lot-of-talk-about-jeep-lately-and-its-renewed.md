## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

So, what makes the Gladiator tick? Under the hood, the Gladiator comes with a range of engine options, including a 3.6-liter V6 and a 3.0-liter diesel.

The V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the diesel engine produces 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque.

The Gladiator also features a range of transmission options, including a six-speed manual and an eight-speed automatic.

When it comes to suspension, the Gladiator features a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

This provides a smooth ride and excellent handling, both on and off the road.
