### Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Ford Bronco comes with a range of engine options, including a 2.3L turbocharged inline-4 cylinder engine and a 2.7L twin-turbocharged V6 engine.

The Bronco also features a advanced suspension system, including a independent front suspension and a solid rear axle.

The vehicle's four-wheel drive system is also highly advanced, with a two-speed electronic transfer case and a locking rear differential.

The following table provides a detailed breakdown of the 2026 Ford Bronco's technical specifications:
| Feature | Description |

| Transmission | 7-speed manual or 10-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Independent front suspension and solid rear axle |
| Four-wheel drive | Two-speed electronic transfer case and locking rear differential |
| Ground clearance | Up to 11.6 inches |
