### Features of the Jeep Wrangler Rubicon 392

The Jeep Wrangler Rubicon 392 is a high-performance off-road vehicle that features a 6.4-liter V-8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. Some of its key features include:
* 33-inch all-terrain tires
* 17-inch bead-lock capable wheels
* Full-time four-wheel drive system
* Electronic locking front and rear differentials
* Disconnecting front sway bar

### Features of the Jeep Gladiator

The Jeep Gladiator is a pickup truck version of the Wrangler, and it is also set to receive a 470-HP V-8 engine. Some of its key features include:
* 33-inch all-terrain tires
* 17-inch aluminum wheels
* Full-time four-wheel drive system
* Electronic locking front and rear differentials
