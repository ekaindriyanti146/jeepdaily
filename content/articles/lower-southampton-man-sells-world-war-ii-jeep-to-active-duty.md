## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Willys MB jeep is powered by a 2.2-liter, 4-cylinder engine, producing 60 horsepower.

Its three-speed manual transmission and two-speed transfer case allow for effective power distribution in various driving conditions.

The jeep's suspension system, featuring a live front axle and a live rear axle with leaf springs, provides the necessary ground clearance and flexibility for off-road driving.

The vehicle's lightweight design, combined with its short wheelbase, enables it to navigate tight spaces and challenging terrains with ease.
