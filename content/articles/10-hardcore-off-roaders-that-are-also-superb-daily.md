## Introduction to Off-Roaders
Off-road vehicles have long been associated with ruggedness and the ability to tackle challenging terrain. However, many modern off-road vehicles have evolved to not only excel in harsh environments but also to provide a comfortable and practical driving experience for daily use. In this article, we'll explore the top 10 hardcore off-roaders that are also superb daily drivers.

### Features of a Daily Driver
When considering an off-roader for daily driving, several features are essential:
* Comfortable interior space
* Advanced safety features
* Smooth on-road handling
* Ample cargo space
* Fuel efficiency
