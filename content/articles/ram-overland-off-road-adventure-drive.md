## Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension of the Ram Overland

The Ram Overland is powered by a range of engine options, including a 3.6L V6, a 5.7L V8, and a 3.0L EcoDiesel V6. Each engine is paired with an advanced eight-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and seamless shifting. The truck's suspension system features a coil-link setup in the front and a five-link setup in the rear, providing excellent articulation and stability. The Ram Overland also features a range of advanced off-road technologies, including a two-speed transfer case, a rear locking differential, and a hill descent control system.
