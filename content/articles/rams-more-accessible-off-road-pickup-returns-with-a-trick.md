## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

So, what makes the Ram Rebel so special? For starters, it's powered by a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 305 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque.

This is paired with an advanced 8-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting, even in the most challenging off-road conditions.

But it's not just the engine that makes the Rebel so capable – it's also the advanced suspension system.

With a combination of coil-link suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension in the rear, the Rebel is able to absorb even the toughest off-road terrain with ease.

And with a ground clearance of 10.3 inches, you can tackle even the most daunting obstacles with confidence.

Here are some key technical details:
| Feature | Specification |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 3.6-liter V6 |
| Horsepower | 305 hp |
| Torque | 269 lb-ft |
| Transmission | 8-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Coil-link front, five-link coil rear |
| Ground Clearance | 10.3 inches |
| Approach Angle | 34.2 degrees |
| Departure Angle | 29.6 degrees |
