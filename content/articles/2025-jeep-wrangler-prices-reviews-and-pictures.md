## Why the Rock-Trac System Changes Everything
      The 2025 Jeep Wrangler is set to revolutionize the off-roading scene with its powerful Rock-Trac system. This system, available in the Rubicon trim, boasts a two-speed transfer case with a 4:1 low-range gear ratio, giving drivers unparalleled control and traction on even the most challenging terrain. Paired with the Wrangler's impressive 33.5 inches of ground clearance and 35-inch tires, this vehicle is the ultimate off-roading machine.

      ## Tackling Moab with 35-Inch Tires
      For those who dare to take on the infamous Moab trails, the 2025 Jeep Wrangler is more than equipped to handle the task. With its 35-inch tires and Rock-Trac system, this vehicle can tackle even the most daunting obstacles with ease. The Wrangler's 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, provides the necessary power to overcome any challenge that comes its way.

      ## JL and JT Models: A Comparison
      The 2025 Jeep Wrangler is available in both JL and JT models, each with its unique features and capabilities. The JL model boasts a more aggressive exterior design, with a wider stance and more angular lines, while the JT model offers a more refined interior, with premium materials and advanced technology features. Both models, however, share the same commitment to off-roading excellence, with the Rock-Trac system and 35-inch tires available in select trims.

      ## Willys MB and TJ Heritage
      The 2025 Jeep Wrangler pays homage to its rich heritage, with nods to the classic Willys MB and TJ models. The Willys MB, the original Jeep, set the standard for off-roading excellence, and the TJ model continued this tradition with its rugged design and impressive capabilities. The 2025 Wrangler builds upon this legacy, with its advanced technology features and refined design, while still maintaining the same spirit of adventure and off-roading prowess.

      ## JK Model Upgrades
      For those who already own a JK model, the 2025 Jeep Wrangler offers several upgrade options to bring their vehicle up to date. The Rock-Trac system and 35-inch tires can be retrofitted onto select JK models, giving owners the same level of off-roading capability as the latest Wrangler. Additionally, the JK model can be upgraded with advanced technology features, such as a touchscreen infotainment system and blind-spot monitoring, to enhance the overall driving experience.

      ## Pricing and Trim Levels
      The 2025 Jeep Wrangler is available in several trim levels, each with its unique features and capabilities. The base Sport trim starts at $28,000, while the top-of-the-line Rubicon trim starts at $43,000. The Rock-Trac system and 35-inch tires are available in select trims, including the Rubicon and Sahara models. With its impressive capabilities and advanced technology features, the 2025 Jeep Wrangler is an unbeatable value in the off-roading market.

      * Engine: 3.6-liter V6

      * Ground Clearance: 33.5 inches
      * Tire Size: 35 inches
      * Transfer Case: Two-speed with 4:1 low-range gear ratio
