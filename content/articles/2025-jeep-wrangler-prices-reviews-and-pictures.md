## Introduction to the 2025 Jeep Wrangler
The 2025 Jeep Wrangler lineup includes several trim levels, each catering to different needs and preferences. From the base Sport model to the high-end Rubicon, there's a Wrangler for every off-road enthusiast.

### Trim Levels and Features
- Sport: The entry-level trim, equipped with essential features like a 3.6L V6 engine and Command-Trac 4x4 system.
- Sport S: Adds features such as 17-inch aluminum wheels and a leather-wrapped steering wheel.
- Sahara: Includes premium amenities like a 7-inch touchscreen display and available 18-inch wheels.
- Rubicon: The off-road enthusiast's dream, featuring a Rock-Trac 4x4 system, 33-inch all-terrain tires, and more.
