## History and Context of the Jeep Wrangler

The Jeep Wrangler has a rich history dating back to 1941, when the first Willys MB was produced.

Over the years, the Wrangler has evolved to become one of the most recognizable and beloved off-road vehicles in the world.

The Rubicon trim, introduced in 2003, is a testament to the Wrangler's off-road prowess, with features such as locking differentials, a disconnecting front sway bar, and 33-inch tires.

The Gladiator, introduced in 2019, is a pickup truck version of the Wrangler, offering the same off-road capabilities with the added practicality of a truck bed.

The 2026 updates aim to build on this legacy, with a focus on improved performance, comfort, and technology.
