## Section 2: Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys '41 Buzz

The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys '41 Buzz model is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting. The vehicle also features a four-wheel-drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and a rear axle with a 3.73 ratio. The suspension system consists of a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear. This setup provides a smooth ride and excellent off-road capability.
