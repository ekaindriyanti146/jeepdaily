## Section 1: History and Context of Willys

Willys, an American brand, has its roots dating back to the early 20th century.

Founded by John Willys, the company initially focused on producing automobiles but later shifted its attention to manufacturing military vehicles during World War II.

The Willys MB, also known as the Jeep, became an iconic symbol of durability and versatility, playing a crucial role in the war effort.
