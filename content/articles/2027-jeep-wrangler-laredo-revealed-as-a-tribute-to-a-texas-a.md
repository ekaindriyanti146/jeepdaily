## Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension

The 2027 Jeep Wrangler Laredo is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting. The Wrangler Laredo also features a robust suspension system, which includes a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear. This suspension setup allows for impressive articulation and flexibility, making it ideal for tackling challenging off-road terrain.
