## History and Context of Off-Road Jacks

Off-road jacks have been around for decades, with the first models being simple, manual devices that required a lot of effort and elbow grease to operate.

Over the years, however, technology has advanced, and modern off-road jacks have become more sophisticated, efficient, and safe.

The introduction of hydraulic and pneumatic systems has made it possible to lift heavy vehicles with ease, while the use of durable materials and clever designs has improved the overall reliability and performance of these devices.

In recent years, the rise of off-road enthusiasts and the growing popularity of recreational vehicles such as ATVs, UTVs, and trucks have created a demand for high-quality, affordable off-road jacks that can keep up with the demands of extreme terrain and heavy use.
