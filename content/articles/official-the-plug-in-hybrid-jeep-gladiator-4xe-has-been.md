The Gladiator 4XE would have also featured a unique "E-Selec" mode, which would have allowed drivers to choose between three different driving modes: Electric, Hybrid, and eSave. The Electric mode would have used only the electric motor, providing up to 25 miles of electric-only range. The Hybrid mode would have combined the gasoline engine and electric motor for optimal fuel efficiency. The eSave mode would have reserved the battery charge for later use, allowing drivers to save the electric range for when it's needed most.

The cancellation of the Jeep Gladiator 4XE is a significant blow to the electric vehicle market, as it would have been one of the first plug-in hybrid pickups available.
