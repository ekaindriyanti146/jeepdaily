## Section 1: History and Context of Jeep's Off-Roaders

Jeep's journey began during World War II, with the Willys MB being the first mass-produced 4x4 vehicle designed for military use.

This early model laid the foundation for what would become a legacy of producing some of the world's most capable off-road vehicles.

Over the years, Jeep has continuously pushed the envelope, introducing models like the CJ, Wrangler, and Grand Cherokee, each with its unique capabilities and features tailored for off-road adventures.

The brand's dedication to off-road prowess has earned it a loyal following and critical acclaim.

This latest milestone is a culmination of years of research, development, and feedback from the off-road community, reflecting Jeep's relentless pursuit of excellence in off-road capability.
