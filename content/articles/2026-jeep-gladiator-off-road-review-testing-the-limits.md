## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Gladiator is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The truck also features a range of advanced suspension systems, including a five-link coil suspension and a disconnecting front sway bar.

This allows for improved articulation and stability, making it ideal for navigating challenging off-road terrain.

Additionally, the Gladiator features a range of advanced technology features, including a touchscreen infotainment system, Bluetooth connectivity, and a range of safety features, such as forward collision warning and blind spot monitoring.
