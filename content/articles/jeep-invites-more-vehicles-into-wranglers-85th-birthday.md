## History/Context

The Jeep Wrangler has a long and storied history that dates back to 1941, when the first Willys MB Jeep was produced.

The Willys MB was designed to meet the needs of the US military, which required a vehicle that could withstand the rigors of war and navigate challenging terrain.

Over the years, the Wrangler has undergone numerous transformations, with each new generation building on the success of the previous one.

In 1986, the Wrangler was introduced as a civilian version of the military Jeep, and it quickly gained popularity among off-road enthusiasts.

Today, the Wrangler is available in a range of trim levels, including the Sport, Sport S, and Rubicon, each with its own unique features and capabilities.

## Real World Performance

The Jeep Wrangler is designed to deliver exceptional off-road performance, and it does not disappoint.
