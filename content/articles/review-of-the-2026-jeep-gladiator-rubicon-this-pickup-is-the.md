## FAQ

### Q: What is the starting price of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Rubicon?

A: The starting price of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Rubicon is around $45,000.

### Q: What is the fuel economy of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Rubicon?

A: The fuel economy of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Rubicon is estimated to be around 17 mpg in the city and 23 mpg on the highway.

### Q: What is the towing capacity of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Rubicon?

A: The towing capacity of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Rubicon is up to 7,650 pounds, depending on the trim level and configuration.
