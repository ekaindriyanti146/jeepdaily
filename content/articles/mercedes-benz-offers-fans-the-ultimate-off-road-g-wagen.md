## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The G-Wagen is powered by a range of engines, including a 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine that produces 577 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission and a two-speed transfer case, allowing for seamless shifting between on-road and off-road modes. The G-Wagen also features a range of advanced suspension systems, including a double-wishbone front suspension and a rear axle with a Panhard rod. The vehicle's suspension is designed to provide exceptional articulation and stability, allowing it to tackle even the most challenging off-road terrain.
