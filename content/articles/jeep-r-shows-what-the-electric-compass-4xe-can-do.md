The world of electric vehicles has witnessed a significant surge in recent years, with numerous automobile manufacturers joining the bandwagon.

## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood, the Compass 4xe boasts a powerful electric motor, paired with a 1.3-liter turbocharged engine.

This hybrid powertrain produces an impressive 177 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, allowing the vehicle to tackle even the most challenging terrain with ease.

The Compass 4xe also features a sophisticated suspension system, which includes frequency-sensitive damping and a 1-inch increase in ride height.

This, combined with the vehicle's advanced four-wheel-drive system, enables the Compass 4xe to navigate through rocky trails, steep inclines, and water crossings with remarkable agility and control.

In real-world conditions, the Compass 4xe has proven to be a formidable off-road vehicle.

During the test in Sardinia, the vehicle effortlessly navigated through rocky trails, steep inclines, and water crossings.
