### 4x4 Systems: The Heart of the Willys 392
The Willys 392 features a robust 4x4 system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and a heavy-duty axle. This system allows the vehicle to seamlessly transition between two-wheel and four-wheel drive, providing optimal traction and control on various surfaces. The Selec-Trac four-wheel-drive system is particularly noteworthy, as it enables the driver to switch between different modes, including a full-time four-wheel-drive mode for improved stability and traction.

#### Technical Breakdown: Transfer Case and Axle
The transfer case is the brain of the 4x4 system, responsible for distributing power between the front and rear axles. The Willys 392 features a heavy-duty transfer case, which is designed to withstand the rigors of off-road driving. The axle, on the other hand, is a critical component that transmits power from the transfer case to the wheels. The Willys 392 boasts a heavy-duty axle, which is capable of handling the stresses of extreme off-roading.

## Articulation and Suspension Tech: The Key to Off-Road Supremacy
The Willys 392 features a suspension system that is specifically designed for off-road applications. The vehicle's articulation is exceptional, thanks to its high ground clearance and flexible suspension components. This allows the Willys 392 to maintain contact with the ground, even on the most uneven terrain, providing optimal traction and control.
