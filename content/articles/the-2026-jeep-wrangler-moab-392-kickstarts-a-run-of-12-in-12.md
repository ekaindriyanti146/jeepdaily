## Technical Details of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 is equipped with a powerful 6.4L V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This potent powerplant is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and responsive shifting.

The Moab 392 also features a unique suspension system, designed to enhance its already impressive off-road capabilities.

With a raised suspension and 33-inch all-terrain tires, this special edition Wrangler is ready to tackle even the most challenging terrain.

Inside, the Moab 392 boasts premium amenities, including leather-trimmed seats, a state-of-the-art infotainment system, and a suite of advanced safety features.
