## Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension

Under the hood, the 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 boasts a massive 6.4-liter V8 engine, capable of producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This powerhouse of an engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting.

The vehicle's suspension system has been upgraded to include a hydraulic shock absorber, which helps to absorb bumps and shocks, providing a smoother ride on both on- and off-road terrain.

The Moab 392 also features a range of advanced technologies, including a disconnecting front sway bar, which allows for increased articulation and flexibility when navigating challenging off-road terrain.
