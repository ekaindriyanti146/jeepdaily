## Section 1: History/Context

The Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator have a long and storied history, dating back to the Willys MB of World War II.

Over the years, these vehicles have undergone numerous transformations, from the CJ-5 to the modern-day Wrangler and Gladiator.

However, with the Rewind edition, Jeep has taken a bold step back in time, drawing inspiration from the classic designs of the 1980s and 1990s.

The result is a pair of vehicles that are both nostalgic and modern, with a unique blend of retro styling and cutting-edge technology.

From the distinctive grille and round headlights to the bold color schemes and vintage-inspired interior trim, every aspect of the Wrangler and Gladiator Rewind has been carefully crafted to evoke a sense of nostalgia and adventure.
