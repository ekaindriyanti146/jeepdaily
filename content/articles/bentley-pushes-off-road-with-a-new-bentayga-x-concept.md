## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Bentayga 'X' Concept is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that produces 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque.

This powerful engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and a permanent all-wheel-drive system, which provides exceptional traction and control on both on-road and off-road terrain.

The suspension system has been upgraded to provide improved ground clearance and articulation, allowing the vehicle to navigate even the most challenging off-road terrain with ease.

Some of the key technical details of the Bentayga 'X' Concept include:
