# Introduction to the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Shadow Ops Special Edition

The Jeep Gladiator has long been a favorite among off-road enthusiasts, known for its rugged capability and versatility. For 2026, Jeep is taking the Gladiator to the next level with the introduction of the Shadow Ops Special Edition, the first mission in the 'Convoy' campaign. This special edition is designed to offer a unique blend of style, capability, and technology, making it the ultimate off-road vehicle.

## Key Features of the Shadow Ops Special Edition

The Shadow Ops Special Edition is packed with features that make it stand out from the standard Gladiator. Some of the key features include:
* Unique exterior design elements, such as a black roof and hood, and exclusive 17-inch black aluminum wheels
* Advanced off-road technology, including a two-speed transfer case and a disconnecting front sway bar
* Premium interior amenities, such as leather-trimmed seats and a 8.4-inch touchscreen display
* A range of safety features, including forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control

The Shadow Ops Special Edition is designed to tackle even the toughest off-road terrain. With its advanced four-wheel-drive system and high-clearance suspension, this vehicle can handle everything from rocky trails to sandy dunes. The Shadow Ops Special Edition also features a range of off-road-specific technologies, including:
