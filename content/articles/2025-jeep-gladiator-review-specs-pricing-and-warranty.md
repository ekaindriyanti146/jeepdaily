## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2025 Jeep Gladiator is powered by a range of engine options, including a 3.6-liter V6 engine and a 3.0-liter EcoDiesel engine.

The V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the EcoDiesel engine produces 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque.

The Gladiator also features a advanced suspension system that includes a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

The truck also features a range of advanced technology features, including a 8.4-inch touchscreen display, a premium audio system, and a suite of safety features.
