## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The America250 Wrangler is based on the standard Wrangler Rubicon, with a number of unique design elements that set it apart.

The vehicle features a 3.6L V6 engine, which produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, which provides smooth and precise shifting.

The America250 Wrangler also features a range of off-road-capable components, including a Rock-Trac four-wheel-drive system, a disconnecting front sway bar, and 33-inch all-terrain tires.

The vehicle's suspension system has been upgraded to provide improved articulation and stability, making it capable of handling even the toughest off-road terrain.
