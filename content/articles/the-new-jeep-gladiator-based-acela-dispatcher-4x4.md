## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Acela Dispatcher 4x4 boasts a powerful 3.6L V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, ensuring that it has the muscle needed for demanding off-road adventures.

This engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and responsive gear shifts.

The vehicle features a four-wheel-drive system, complete with a two-speed transfer case, allowing for seamless transitions between two-wheel drive and four-wheel drive.

The suspension system is specially tuned for off-road use, with a focus on providing maximum articulation and stability over rough terrain.
