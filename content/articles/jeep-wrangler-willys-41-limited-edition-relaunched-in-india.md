## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Wrangler Willys 41 Limited Edition is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged petrol engine, which produces 268 horsepower and 400 Nm of torque.

The engine is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting.

The vehicle features a four-wheel-drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and a rear locking differential.

The suspension system consists of a five-link setup with coil springs and gas-filled shock absorbers.

The Willys 41 Limited Edition also features a range of modern technologies, including a touchscreen infotainment system, a rearview camera, and a suite of advanced safety features.
