## Hook

The automotive world has seen a significant shift towards automatic transmissions in recent years, with many manufacturers discontinuing manual options from their lineups.

However, for driving enthusiasts, the joy of shifting gears manually remains unparalleled.

In 2026, amidst this trend, the Jeep Wrangler stands out as the cheapest manual transmission SUV you can buy.

But what makes the Wrangler so special, and how does it balance affordability with the off-road prowess and manual transmission that its loyal fan base demands? Let's dive into the world of the Jeep Wrangler and explore its unique blend of capability, tradition, and value.

## Section 1: History/Context

The Jeep Wrangler has a rich history that dates back to World War II, where the Willys MB, the original Jeep, was born out of necessity.

Over the years, the Wrangler has evolved, maintaining its core identity as a rugged, off-road capable vehicle.

The inclusion of a manual transmission has been a staple of the Wrangler experience, offering drivers a level of control and engagement that automatics can't match.

The Wrangler's ability to balance this heritage with modern comforts and capabilities has made it a standout in the SUV market.

## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Technically, the Jeep Wrangler is equipped with a range of engines, including a 3.6-liter V6 and a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four, both of which can be paired with a six-speed manual transmission.

The Wrangler's suspension is designed for off-road capability, featuring a body-on-frame design and solid axles front and rear.

This setup, combined with the manual transmission, provides a driving experience that is both engaging on the road and capable off it.

The Wrangler also comes with a variety of trim levels, from the base Sport to the more luxurious Rubicon, each offering a unique set of features and capabilities.

For those looking for the ultimate in off-road performance, the Rubicon model, with its locking differentials and disconnecting front sway bar, is the pinnacle of Wrangler capability.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

In real-world driving, the Jeep Wrangler with a manual transmission delivers on its promise of engagement and capability.

The six-speed manual transmission is smooth and easy to use, making it accessible to both new and experienced drivers.

Off the road, the Wrangler's suspension and four-wheel-drive system work in harmony to tackle challenging terrain with ease.

Whether navigating rocky trails or simply commuting to work, the Wrangler offers a unique driving experience that is hard to find in other SUVs.

Its manual transmission is not just a nod to its past but a functional choice that enhances the overall driving experience.

## Section 4: Comparison/Verdict

When comparing the Jeep Wrangler to other SUVs on the market, its manual transmission option stands out as a unique selling point. While other manufacturers may offer manual transmissions in certain models, the Wrangler's combination of off-road capability, modern comforts, and manual transmission make it a compelling choice for those who value driving engagement. In conclusion, the Jeep Wrangler is not just the cheapest manual transmission SUV you can buy in 2026; it's a vehicle that embodies a spirit of adventure and driving enthusiasm that is hard to find elsewhere.

### FAQs

1. **Is the Jeep Wrangler available with an automatic transmission?**

- Yes, the Jeep Wrangler is available with an automatic transmission, but for those who prefer the engagement of a manual, the six-speed manual is an option across various trim levels.

2. **What is the starting price of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler with a manual transmission?**

- The starting price for a 2026 Jeep Wrangler with a manual transmission begins at $28,975 for the base Sport model.

3. **Does the Jeep Wrangler come in a four-door version with a manual transmission?**

- Yes, the Jeep Wrangler Unlimited (four-door) is available with a manual transmission, offering the same level of driving engagement as the two-door model but with additional space and practicality.

4. **Can I get a manual transmission with the Wrangler's turbocharged engine?**

- Yes, the 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine in the Jeep Wrangler can be paired with a six-speed manual transmission, offering a blend of performance and efficiency.
