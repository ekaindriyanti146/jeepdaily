## Section 3: Real World Performance

So how do these vehicles perform in real-world off-road conditions? The answer is impressive, with each model capable of tackling challenging terrain with ease. The Toyota 4Runner, for example, features a robust four-wheel drive system and a locking rear differential, making it well-suited to rocky and sandy terrain. The Land Rover Defender, on the other hand, features a wade-sensing system, which allows it to ford water up to 35.4 inches deep.

## Section 4: Comparison/Verdict

So which of these vehicles is the best off-road capable SUV available in the USA? The answer ultimately depends on your specific needs and budget. If you're looking for a rugged and reliable vehicle for extreme off-road use, the Jeep Wrangler or Toyota 4Runner may be the best choice.
