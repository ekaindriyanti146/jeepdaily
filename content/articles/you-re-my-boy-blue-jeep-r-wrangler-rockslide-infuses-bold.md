## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Wrangler Rockslide is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, allowing for precise control and smooth shifting.

The vehicle's suspension system features a five-link coil setup in the front and a five-link leaf spring setup in the rear, providing a smooth ride and excellent articulation.

The Rockslide also features a range of advanced technology features, including a Uconnect infotainment system and a range of safety features such as electronic stability control and anti-lock braking.
