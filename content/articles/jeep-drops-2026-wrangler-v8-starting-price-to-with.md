# Introduction to the 2026 Jeep Wrangler V8
The Jeep Wrangler is an iconic off-road vehicle known for its ruggedness and versatility. For the 2026 model year, Jeep has introduced the Wrangler V8, starting at $71,990, and featuring the new Willys 392 edition. This update brings significant changes to the lineup, including a more affordable entry point for the V8-powered Wrangler.

## Key Features of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler V8
### Engine and Performance
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler V8 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. This significant boost in power enhances the vehicle's off-road capabilities and on-road performance.

### Trim Levels and Pricing
The Wrangler V8 is available in several trim levels, including the Willys 392, Rubicon 392, and Mojave 392. The starting price of $71,990 for the Willys 392 edition makes it an attractive option for those seeking a V8-powered Wrangler without breaking the bank.

| Trim Level | Starting Price |
| --- | --- |
| Willys 392 | $71,990 |
| Rubicon 392 | $79,990 |
| Mojave 392 | $83,990 |

## Features and Options
Some notable features of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler V8 include:
* 6.4-liter V8 engine
* 8-speed automatic transmission
* Four-wheel drive with a two-speed transfer case
* 33-inch all-terrain tires
* Uconnect 4C infotainment system with an 8.4-inch touchscreen
* Forward-facing camera for off-road navigation
* Available options like a leather-wrapped steering wheel and aluminum wheels

### Off-Road Capabilities
The Jeep Wrangler V8 is designed to excel in off-road environments, with features such as:
* High and low range gearing
* Locking front and rear differentials
* Skid plates for protection
* Water fording capability up to 32.5 inches

## Conclusion
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler V8, with its new Willys 392 edition, offers an exciting combination of power, capability, and affordability. Starting at $71,990, it provides a more accessible entry point for those seeking a V8-powered Wrangler. With its robust engine, advanced technology features, and legendary off-road prowess, the 2026 Jeep Wrangler V8 is sure to appeal to both new and seasoned Jeep enthusiasts.
