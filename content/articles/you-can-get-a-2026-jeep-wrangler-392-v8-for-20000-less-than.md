## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler 392 V8 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This potent powerplant is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, allowing for seamless shifting and optimal power delivery.

The Wrangler's suspension system features a heavy-duty Dana 44 axle in the front and a Dana 44 axle in the rear, providing exceptional articulation and stability on uneven terrain.

The vehicle's ground clearance is an impressive 10.3 inches, allowing it to tackle challenging off-road obstacles with ease.
