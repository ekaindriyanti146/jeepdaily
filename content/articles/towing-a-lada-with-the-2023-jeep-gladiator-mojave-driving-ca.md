## Technical Details and Engine

The 2023 Jeep Gladiator Mojave is equipped with a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. This, combined with its eight-speed automatic transmission and four-wheel drive system, makes it an ideal vehicle for towing smaller vehicles like the Lada. The Gladiator Mojave's suspension system is also noteworthy, featuring a heavy-duty setup with FOX shocks and a reinforced frame that allows for improved stability and control when towing.

The Gladiator Mojave's towing capacity is impressive, with a maximum rating of up to 7,650 pounds. This, combined with its advanced technology features like trailer sway control and hill descent control, makes it an excellent choice for towing smaller vehicles like the Lada.

In real-world testing, the 2023 Jeep Gladiator Mojave performed admirably when towing the Lada. The Gladiator Mojave's towing capabilities were also put to the test on off-road terrain, where it performed flawlessly, navigating rocky trails and steep inclines with ease.
