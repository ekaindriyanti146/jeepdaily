The Wrangler's suspension system is designed to provide a smooth ride on-road, while also being capable of handling extreme off-road terrain.

The vehicle's suspension travel is impressive, with up to 10.9 inches of articulation in the front and 10.8 inches in the rear.

In real-world testing, the Jeep Wrangler has proven to be an impressive off-road performer.
