# Introduction to the 2026 Wrangler Willys 392
The 2026 Wrangler Willys 392 is the latest addition to the Jeep family, offering a powerful V-8 engine at a lower price point than its predecessors. This new model is set to excite Jeep enthusiasts and potential buyers looking for a capable and affordable SUV.

## Key Features of the 2026 Wrangler Willys 392
### Engine and Performance
The 2026 Wrangler Willys 392 is powered by a 6.4-liter V-8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and responsive shifting.

### Exterior and Interior Design
The exterior design of the 2026 Wrangler Willys 392 features a rugged and aggressive look, with a distinctive front grille and hood scoop. The interior is equipped with premium materials, including leather-trimmed seats and a touchscreen infotainment system.

### Off-Road Capability
The 2026 Wrangler Willys 392 is designed to handle challenging off-road terrain, with features such as:
* Four-wheel drive with a two-speed transfer case
* 33-inch all-terrain tires
* Heavy-duty suspension with gas-filled shock absorbers
* Water fording capability up to 30 inches
