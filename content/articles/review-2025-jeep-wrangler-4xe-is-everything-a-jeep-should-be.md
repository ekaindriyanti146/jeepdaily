The Wrangler, in particular, has been a favorite among off-road enthusiasts since its introduction in 1986.

## Technical Details

The 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe is a plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) that combines a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine with two electric motors.

The engine produces 270 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, while the electric motors add an additional 90 horsepower and 130 lb-ft of torque.

The total system output is 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque, making the 4xe one of the most powerful Wranglers ever produced.

The 4xe also features a robust four-wheel-drive system, complete with a two-speed transfer case and a locking rear differential.

The suspension is equally impressive, with a heavy-duty coil-link suspension in the front and a multi-link suspension in the rear.

The 4xe also features a range of advanced technology, including an 8.4-inch touchscreen display, a 7-inch driver information display, and a suite of safety features, including forward collision warning and blind-spot monitoring.

## Real World Performance

The 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe is a beast on the road and off.

With its powerful engine and electric motors, the 4xe can accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 6.0 seconds, making it one of the quickest Wranglers ever produced.

The 4xe also features a range of driving modes, including Hybrid, Electric, and eSave, which allow drivers to customize their driving experience to suit their needs.

In Electric mode, the 4xe can travel up to 25 miles on electric power alone, making it an excellent option for daily driving.

Off-road, the 4xe is equally impressive, with its robust four-wheel-drive system and heavy-duty suspension allowing it to tackle even the most challenging terrain with ease.
