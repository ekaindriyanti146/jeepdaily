## What is a Tray-Bed Conversion?

A tray-bed conversion involves replacing the traditional bed of the Jeep Gladiator with a flat, tray-like surface. This design provides a clean slate for customization, allowing owners to add their preferred camping gear, storage solutions, and other accessories.

### Benefits of a Tray-Bed Conversion

The benefits of a tray-bed conversion are numerous:
* Increased storage capacity
* Improved organization and accessibility
* Enhanced durability and protection
* Customization options for personalized setups
