## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Gladiator is equipped with a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission.

The Gladiator also features a robust suspension system that includes a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

The suspension system is designed to provide a smooth ride and excellent handling, both on and off the road.

The Gladiator's towing capacity is impressive, with a maximum capacity of up to 7,650 pounds.
