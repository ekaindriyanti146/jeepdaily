## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood, the new Gladiator option is powered by a range of capable engines, including a 3.6-liter V6 and a 3.0-liter EcoDiesel V6. These engines provide the perfect blend of power and efficiency, making the Gladiator suitable for everything from daily driving to serious off-road adventure.
