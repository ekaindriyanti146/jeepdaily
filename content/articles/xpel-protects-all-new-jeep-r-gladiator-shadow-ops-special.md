## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood, the 2026 Jeep Gladiator 'Shadow Ops' Special Edition packs a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, allowing for seamless shifting and optimal performance.

The truck's suspension system features a five-link coil setup in the front and a five-link leaf spring setup in the rear, providing a smooth ride and exceptional off-road articulation.

XPEL's protection solutions include a comprehensive paint protection film, as well as a range of other protective coatings designed to safeguard the truck's exterior and interior surfaces.
