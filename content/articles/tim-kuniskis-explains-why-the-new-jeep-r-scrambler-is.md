## Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension of the New Jeep Scrambler

At the heart of the new Jeep Scrambler is a powerful engine, designed to deliver exceptional performance both on and off the road.

The vehicle is equipped with a robust 3.6-liter V6 engine, capable of producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This potent powerplant is paired with a sophisticated eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides seamless shifting and optimal power delivery.

The Scrambler's suspension system is equally impressive, featuring a durable and responsive design that can handle even the most challenging terrain.

The vehicle's advanced four-wheel-drive system, complete with a two-speed transfer case and heavy-duty axles, ensures that power is distributed efficiently to all four wheels, maximizing traction and stability.
