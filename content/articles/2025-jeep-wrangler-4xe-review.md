This move is a testament to Jeep's commitment to innovation and sustainability. The 4xe is not just a vehicle; it's a statement about the future of off-roading and the role that electric vehicles will play in it.

The Wrangler 4xe is built on the same platform as the traditional Wrangler, but it features a number of significant upgrades. The most notable of these is the plug-in hybrid-electric powertrain, which combines a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine with an electric motor. ## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe is a technological marvel. Its plug-in hybrid-electric powertrain is the result of years of research and development, and it shows. The vehicle features a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine, which is paired with an electric motor and a high-voltage battery pack. This setup provides an estimated 25 miles of electric-only range, making it perfect for short trips around town or quiet moments on the trail.

The Wrangler 4xe also features a number of advanced technologies, including an eight-speed automatic transmission and a Selec-Trac four-wheel-drive system. This system allows drivers to switch between two-wheel drive, four-wheel drive, and electric-only modes, giving them unparalleled control over their vehicle.

The suspension on the Wrangler 4xe is just as impressive as its powertrain. The vehicle features a heavy-duty suspension system, which includes upgraded shocks and springs. This setup provides a smooth ride on the pavement and exceptional articulation on the trail.
