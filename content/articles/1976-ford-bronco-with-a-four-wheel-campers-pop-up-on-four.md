## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 1976 Ford Bronco features a robust 351 CID V8 engine, paired with a three-speed automatic transmission.

The engine produces 168 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, making it well-suited for off-road adventures.

The Bronco's four-wheel-drive system features a two-speed transfer case, allowing drivers to switch between two-wheel and four-wheel drive.

The suspension system consists of a coil-spring front axle and a leaf-spring rear axle, providing a smooth ride and ample ground clearance.

The Four Wheel Campers Pop-Up is designed to be lightweight and compact, making it easy to install and remove as needed.

The pop-up features a comfortable sleeping area, a compact kitchenette, and ample storage space.
