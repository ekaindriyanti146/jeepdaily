The Jeep Gladiator is a mid-size pickup truck that was first introduced in 2019. It's based on the same platform as the Jeep Wrangler, but with a longer wheelbase and a pickup bed. The Gladiator is designed to be a capable off-road vehicle, with features like four-wheel drive, a two-speed transfer case, and a range of axle ratios. The truck is available in several trim levels, including the Sport, Sport S, Overland, and Rubicon. Each trim level offers a unique set of features and capabilities, allowing buyers to choose the model that best fits their needs.

## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood, the Gladiator is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission.

The truck also features a range of suspension options, including a heavy-duty suspension system with FOX shocks.

The Gladiator's suspension is designed to provide a smooth ride on-road, while also being capable of handling rough off-road terrain.

The truck's approach and departure angles are also impressive, with an approach angle of up to 43.6 degrees and a departure angle of up to 26 degrees.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

In the real world, the Gladiator performs exceptionally well both on-road and off-road. The truck's V6 engine provides plenty of power for merging onto highways and towing trailers, while the suspension system soaks up bumps and rough terrain with ease. Off-road, the Gladiator is a beast, with its four-wheel drive system and high ground clearance allowing it to tackle even the toughest trails. The truck's approach and departure angles are also impressive, making it capable of handling steep inclines and declines.

## Section 4: Comparison/Verdict

Compared to other mid-size pickup trucks on the market, the Gladiator stands out for its impressive off-road capabilities and rugged design. While it may not be the most refined or comfortable truck on the market, the Gladiator makes up for it with its exceptional performance and capability. The truck's starting price of $35,040 is also competitive with other models in its class. Overall, the 2020 Jeep Gladiator is a great option for those looking for a capable and rugged off-road vehicle.
