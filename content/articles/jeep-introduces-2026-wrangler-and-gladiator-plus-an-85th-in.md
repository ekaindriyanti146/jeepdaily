## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Wrangler and Gladiator are equipped with a range of powerful engines, including a 3.6L V6 and a 2.0L turbocharged inline-four cylinder. The Wrangler boasts a maximum towing capacity of up to 3,500 pounds, while the Gladiator can tow up to 7,650 pounds. Both vehicles feature an advanced suspension system, including a five-link coil suspension and a disconnecting front sway bar, allowing for unparalleled off-road articulation and stability. The following table highlights the key technical specifications of the 2026 Wrangler and Gladiator:

| Model | Engine | Horsepower | Torque | Towing Capacity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Wrangler | 3.6L V6 | 285 HP | 260 lb-ft | Up to 3,500 pounds |
| Wrangler | 2.0L Turbo | 270 HP | 295 lb-ft | Up to 3,500 pounds |
| Gladiator | 3.6L V6 | 285 HP | 260 lb-ft | Up to 7,650 pounds |
| Gladiator | 2.0L Turbo | 270 HP | 295 lb-ft | Up to 7,650 pounds |
