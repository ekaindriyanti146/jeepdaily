## Technical Details of the Jeep Gladiator

The Jeep Gladiator is equipped with a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level. The Gladiator also features a advanced suspension system that includes a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear. This suspension system provides a smooth ride on paved roads and excellent articulation off-road.
