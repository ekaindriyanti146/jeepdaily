## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Ford Everest is powered by a 2.0-liter bi-turbo diesel engine, which produces 154kW of power and 500Nm of torque. This is paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission and a four-wheel-drive system, which provides exceptional traction and control. The Everest's suspension system is also noteworthy, with a coil-spring rear suspension and a Watt's link rear axle, which provides improved stability and articulation. The following table summarizes the key technical details of the Ford Everest:

## Section 3: Real World Performance

The Ford Everest's real-world performance is impressive, with exceptional off-road capability and on-road comfort.
