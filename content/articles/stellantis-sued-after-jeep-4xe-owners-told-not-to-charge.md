## Section 1: History/Context

The Jeep 4xe is a plug-in hybrid version of the popular Jeep Wrangler. It was first introduced in 2020, offering a unique combination of off-road capability and eco-friendliness. The 4xe is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and an electric motor, producing a total system output of 375 horsepower. With its advanced technology and impressive performance, the Jeep 4xe quickly gained popularity among environmentally conscious off-road enthusiasts. However, the recent advisory from Stellantis has raised concerns about the vehicle's reliability and safety.

## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep 4xe features a sophisticated powertrain, consisting of a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine and an electric motor. The engine produces 270 horsepower, while the electric motor contributes an additional 105 horsepower. The vehicle's advanced all-wheel-drive system and high-strength steel frame provide exceptional off-road capability and durability. The 4xe also features an advanced suspension system, including a coil-link suspension in the front and a multi-link suspension in the rear. This setup enables the vehicle to navigate challenging terrain with ease and comfort.
