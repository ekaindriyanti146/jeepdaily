## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Reliability King is powered by a robust 3.0L diesel engine that produces 250 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and a two-speed transfer case, giving you the flexibility to tackle any terrain. The SUV's suspension system features a coil-link rear suspension and a double-wishbone front suspension, providing a smooth ride and excellent articulation. The Reliability King also features a range of advanced technologies, including a terrain management system, hill descent control, and a locking rear differential.
