The vehicle's engine, a 3.8L V6, produces 202 horsepower and 237 lb-ft of torque, providing ample power for navigating challenging terrain.

The Wrangler (JK) also features a Command-Trac part-time four-wheel drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and heavy-duty Dana 30 front and Dana 35 rear axles.

The suspension system, comprising live axles with coil springs and stabilizer bars, offers a smooth ride on-road while maintaining the necessary articulation for off-road adventures.
