# Introduction to the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys ’41
The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys ’41 is a tribute to the original Willys MB, a vehicle that played a significant role in World War II. This new model brings back the M715-style military grit, offering a unique blend of nostalgia and modern capabilities.

## Design and Features
### Exterior Design
The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys ’41 features a rugged and aggressive exterior design, inspired by the original Willys MB. The vehicle has a distinctive front grille, round headlights, and a bold, rectangular shape. The exterior is available in several colors, including a unique military-inspired green.

### Interior Features
The interior of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys ’41 is designed to be functional and comfortable. The vehicle features a range of amenities, including:
* Premium leather seats
* Heated and ventilated front seats
* 8.4-inch touchscreen display
* Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration
* Wi-Fi hotspot

### Off-Road Capabilities
The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys ’41 is designed to be a capable off-road vehicle, with features such as:
* Four-wheel drive system
* High and low range gearing
* Locking front and rear differentials
* 33-inch all-terrain tires

## Performance and Specifications
The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys ’41 is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The vehicle also features an eight-speed automatic transmission and a range of advanced safety features, including forward collision warning and blind spot monitoring.
