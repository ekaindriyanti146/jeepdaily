## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep turbo four engine is a 2.0-liter inline-four cylinder engine that produces 270 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque.

This is comparable to the 3.6-liter V6 engine, which produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The turbo four engine also features a number of advanced technologies, including a twin-scroll turbocharger and a direct fuel injection system.

The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting.

The suspension system has also been upgraded, with a more advanced coil-over shock system that provides better stability and control on uneven terrain.
