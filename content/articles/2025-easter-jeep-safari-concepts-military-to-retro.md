## The Wrangler's Evolution: From CJ to JL
The Easter Jeep Safari has long been a platform for Jeep to showcase its latest and greatest concepts, and the 2025 event is no exception. This year's lineup features a range of vehicles that pay homage to the brand's military heritage, while also highlighting its commitment to innovation and modernization. From the classic CJ to the latest JL, the Wrangler has come a long way, and its evolution is evident in the latest concepts.
## Retro Revival: The YJ and TJ Inspire New Designs
The YJ and TJ models, introduced in the 80s and 90s, respectively, have become iconic in the world of off-roading. Their rugged design and capabilities have inspired a new generation of Jeep enthusiasts, and the latest concepts reflect this. With retro-inspired designs and modern twists, these vehicles are sure to appeal to both old and new fans of the brand.

## Military to Modern: The JK and JT Concepts
