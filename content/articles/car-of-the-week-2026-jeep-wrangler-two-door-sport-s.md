## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler two-door Sport S is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, although an eight-speed automatic is also available as an option. The vehicle's four-wheel-drive system is designed to provide maximum traction and control, with a range of modes to suit different driving conditions.

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler two-door Sport S features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension setup at the front and rear. The vehicle's ground clearance is an impressive 10.8 inches, allowing it to tackle challenging terrain with ease. The Wrangler's approach and departure angles are also noteworthy, with an approach angle of 35.5 degrees and a departure angle of 29.2 degrees.
