## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

At the heart of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 is a potent 6.4-liter V8 engine, capable of producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This significant power output, combined with an advanced eight-speed automatic transmission, enables the vehicle to tackle even the most daunting off-road challenges with ease.

The Moab 392 also features a high-performance suspension system, designed to provide exceptional stability and control, whether navigating rocky terrain or cruising on paved roads.

Other notable technical features include a disconnecting front sway bar, which enhances articulation and flexibility in low-speed, high-torque situations, and a set of 17-inch bead-lock capable wheels wrapped in 33-inch all-terrain tires, ensuring maximum grip and traction on various surfaces.

The vehicle's approach and departure angles, as well as its ground clearance, have been optimized for off-road use, allowing drivers to tackle steep inclines and declines with confidence.
