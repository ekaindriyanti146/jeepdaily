## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

So, what exactly are the engineering tricks that make the Wrangler Rubicon so unstoppable? Let's start with the suspension system.

The Rubicon features a robust suspension system that includes a disconnecting front sway bar, which allows for increased articulation and flexibility when driving over rough terrain.

The vehicle also features a set of heavy-duty axles, which are designed to withstand the stresses and strains of off-road driving.

In terms of engine power, the Rubicon is equipped with a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, which provides smooth and precise shifting.

The Rubicon also features a advanced four-wheel-drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and a set of electronic locking differentials.

This system allows the driver to switch between two-wheel drive and four-wheel drive on the fly, and it also provides increased traction and control when driving over slippery or uneven surfaces.

### FAQ

1.

**What is the towing capacity of the Jeep Wrangler Rubicon?**
The towing capacity of the Jeep Wrangler Rubicon is up to 3,500 pounds, depending on the trim level and configuration.
2.

**Can the Wrangler Rubicon be used for on-road driving?**
Yes, the Wrangler Rubicon can be used for on-road driving, although its off-road biased design may make it less comfortable and less fuel-efficient than other vehicles.
3.

**What is the ground clearance of the Wrangler Rubicon?**
The ground clearance of the Wrangler Rubicon is 10.8 inches, which provides ample clearance for navigating rough off-road terrain.
4.

**Is the Wrangler Rubicon available with an automatic transmission?**
