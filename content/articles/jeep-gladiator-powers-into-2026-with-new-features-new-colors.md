## Section 2: Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator

The 2026 Jeep Gladiator boasts an impressive array of technical features that set it apart from its competitors. The vehicle is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. Additionally, a 3.0-liter EcoDiesel V6 engine is available, producing 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque. The Gladiator's suspension system has been tweaked to provide a smoother ride, both on and off the road. The following table highlights the key technical specifications of the 2026 Jeep Gladiator:

| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 3.6-liter V6, 3.0-liter EcoDiesel V6 |
| Horsepower | 285 hp (3.6L), 260 hp (3.0L) |
| Torque | 260 lb-ft (3.6L), 442 lb-ft (3.0L) |
| Transmission | 6-speed manual, 8-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Heavy-duty shock absorbers, coil-link suspension |
| Ground Clearance | Up to 11.1 inches |
| Towing Capacity | Up to 7,650 pounds |

The 2026 Gladiator also features a range of advanced safety features, including forward collision warning, adaptive cruise control, and a blind-spot monitoring system.
