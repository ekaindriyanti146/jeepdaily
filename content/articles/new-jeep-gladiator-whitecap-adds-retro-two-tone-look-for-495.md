## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Gladiator Whitecap edition is available on the Overland and Rubicon models, and it features a unique two-tone color scheme that includes a white roof and hood, paired with a range of available body colors.

The Whitecap edition also includes a set of 17-inch aluminum wheels with a mid-gloss black finish, as well as a body-color fender flare.

Under the hood, the Gladiator Whitecap edition is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level.

The Gladiator's suspension system is also noteworthy, featuring a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

This setup provides a smooth ride on paved roads and exceptional articulation off-road.
