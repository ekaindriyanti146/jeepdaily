## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Wrangler Rubicon 4-Door Facelift boasts an impressive array of technical specifications, including a 3.6L V6 engine producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The vehicle features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil setup in the front and a five-link leaf spring design in the rear. The Rubicon trim is equipped with 33-inch all-terrain tires, rock rails, and a disconnecting front sway bar, allowing for unparalleled articulation and flexibility. The Jeep Wrangler Rubicon 4-Door Facelift is an absolute beast on the trails, with its potent engine and advanced suspension system working in harmony to tackle even the most daunting obstacles.
