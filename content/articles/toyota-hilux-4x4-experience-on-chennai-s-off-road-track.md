## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Toyota Hilux 4X4 is powered by a 2.8-liter diesel engine, which produces 201 horsepower and 500 Nm of torque.

This engine is paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting.

The vehicle's 4-wheel drive system is highly advanced, with a two-speed transfer case and a locking rear differential.

The suspension is equally impressive, with a double-wishbone setup at the front and a leaf-spring setup at the rear.

This combination of power and suspension makes the Toyota Hilux 4X4 a formidable off-road vehicle, capable of handling even the most challenging terrain.
