# Introduction

The automotive world is abuzz with the latest news from Jeep, as the brand celebrates its 85th anniversary in style. To commemorate this milestone, Jeep has unveiled special 85th Anniversary Editions of its iconic Wrangler and Gladiator models. These limited-edition vehicles boast unique design elements, both inside and out, that set them apart from their standard counterparts. For off-road enthusiasts and Jeep aficionados alike, these special editions are sure to generate significant excitement.

Jeep's history spans over eight decades, with its roots tracing back to the Willys MB, a vehicle that played a crucial role in World War II.

Since then, the brand has evolved significantly, expanding its lineup to include a variety of models that cater to different tastes and preferences.

The Wrangler and Gladiator are two of Jeep's most popular offerings, renowned for their exceptional off-road capability and rugged design.

The introduction of the 85th Anniversary Editions marks a significant moment in Jeep's history, as the brand continues to innovate and push the boundaries of what is possible in the world of off-road vehicles.

## Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension

The 85th Anniversary Editions of the Wrangler and Gladiator come with a range of technical upgrades and unique design elements.

Under the hood, these vehicles are powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, which produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and seamless shifting.

In terms of suspension, the Wrangler and Gladiator feature a robust system that includes heavy-duty shock absorbers and springs, allowing for exceptional articulation and control on uneven terrain.
