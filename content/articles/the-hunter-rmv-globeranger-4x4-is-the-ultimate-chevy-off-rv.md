## Technical Details

The Hunter RMV GLOBeRanger 4X4 is a marvel of modern engineering, boasting an array of impressive technical features.

The vehicle is powered by a 6.6L Duramax turbo-diesel engine, which produces 460 horsepower and 910 lb-ft of torque.

This potent engine is paired with an Allison 6-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and seamless gear shifts.

The GLOBeRanger's suspension system is equally impressive, featuring a custom-designed 4-link suspension with Fox shocks, which provides unparalleled stability and control.

Other notable features include a 46-gallon fuel tank, a 20,000-pound winch, and a state-of-the-art interior with premium amenities.
