## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood of the 2027 Ram 1500 TRX SRT lies a beast of an engine - a supercharged 6.2L V8, capable of producing 702 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque. The suspension system has been thoroughly reworked, with a focus on off-road articulation and stability. The truck features a unique suspension setup, including a five-link coil suspension with Bilstein Blackhawk e2 shocks, providing unparalleled ride quality and stability, both on and off the road.
