## Hook

The Jeep Gladiator is already a beast of a truck, capable of tackling even the toughest off-road terrain. But what if you could make it look like it just rolled out of a classic car show? Enter the new Whitecap Package, a retro-inspired trim that gives the Gladiator a unique and stylish look. With its classic color scheme and vintage-inspired design elements, the Whitecap Package is sure to turn heads on and off the road.

## History/Context

The Jeep Gladiator has a long history of being a rugged and reliable off-road vehicle.

First introduced in 1962, the Gladiator was known for its strength and versatility, making it a popular choice for farmers, construction workers, and outdoor enthusiasts.

Over the years, the Gladiator has undergone many changes, but its core values have remained the same.

The new Whitecap Package is a nod to the Gladiator's rich history, blending classic style with modern off-road performance.

The package is inspired by the classic Jeep trucks of the 1960s and 1970s, with a focus on clean lines, simple design, and a bold color scheme.

## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Gladiator Whitecap Package is more than just a pretty face.

Under the hood, it packs a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic transmission.

The Gladiator's suspension system is designed to handle even the toughest off-road terrain, with a heavy-duty Dana 44 axle in the front and a Dana 44 axle in the rear.

The Whitecap Package also includes a range of standard features, including:
