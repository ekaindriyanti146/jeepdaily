This powerful engine is paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and seamless shifting.

The Tremor also features a unique suspension system, designed to handle the toughest off-road terrain.

The system includes a terrain management system, which allows drivers to choose from several modes, including normal, trail, deep snow, and sand.

The Explorer Tremor also boasts a generous ground clearance of 10.7 inches, making it well-suited for navigating rocky, uneven terrain.

## Real-World Performance of the Ford Explorer Tremor

In real-world testing, the Ford Explorer Tremor has proven to be a formidable off-road vehicle. ## Comparison and Verdict on the Ford Explorer Tremor

The Ford Explorer Tremor is a unique vehicle that combines the best of both worlds: on-road comfort and off-road capability.
