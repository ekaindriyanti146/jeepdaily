# Ryan Newman Returns to Ram at North Wilkesboro

Ryan Newman is making a highly anticipated return to the NASCAR scene, this time behind the wheel of the No. 25 Ram for Kaulig Racing at the historic North Wilkesboro Speedway. The Faith Fest 250, scheduled for Saturday, promises to be an exhilarating event, given the track's unique layout and Newman's wealth of experience.

## History/Context

North Wilkesboro Speedway, with its 0.625-mile oval track, is renowned for its challenging downhill frontstretch and uphill backstretch. This configuration tends to produce very close and physical racing, a scenario that could play to Newman's strengths. His return to the Ram is a significant development, especially considering his extensive background in NASCAR. Newman has competed at the highest levels, including in the Cup Series, and his insights and skillset will undoubtedly be valuable assets to Kaulig Racing.
