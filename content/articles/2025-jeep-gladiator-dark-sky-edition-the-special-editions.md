## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2025 Jeep Gladiator Dark Sky Edition is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting, even in the most demanding off-road conditions.

The suspension system features a five-link coil setup in the front and a five-link leaf spring setup in the rear, providing a perfect balance of on-road comfort and off-road capability.

The Dark Sky Edition also features a range of advanced technology, including a 12-inch touchscreen display, a premium audio system, and a suite of safety features, such as forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control.
