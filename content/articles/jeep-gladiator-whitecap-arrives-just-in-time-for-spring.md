## FAQs

1. **What is the towing capacity of the Jeep Gladiator Whitecap?**

- The Jeep Gladiator Whitecap has a maximum towing capacity of up to 7,650 pounds, depending on the configuration and options chosen.

2. **Does the Gladiator Whitecap come with four-wheel drive as standard?**

- Yes, the Jeep Gladiator Whitecap features a four-wheel-drive system as standard, including a two-speed transfer case for low-range gearing.

3. **Are there any interior upgrades or unique features in the Whitecap edition?**

- The Gladiator Whitecap includes several unique interior features, such as premium cloth seats, a leather-wrapped steering wheel, and an 8.4-inch touchscreen display with navigation.

4. **Can the Gladiator Whitecap be ordered with a manual transmission?**

- Unfortunately, the Gladiator Whitecap is only available with an 8-speed automatic transmission; there is no manual transmission option for this model.
