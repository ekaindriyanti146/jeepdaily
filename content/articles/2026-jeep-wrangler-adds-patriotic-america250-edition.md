## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The America250 Edition is based on the Wrangler Rubicon, which means it inherits the Rubicon's capable four-wheel-drive system and robust suspension. The Wrangler's suspension is designed to handle the toughest off-road terrain, with a combination of coil springs, linkages, and stabilizer bars. The America250 Edition also features a range of advanced technologies, including a disconnecting front sway bar, which allows for greater articulation and flexibility when driving off-road.

The America250 Edition is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, which produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic. The Wrangler's four-wheel-drive system features a two-speed transfer case, which allows drivers to switch between two-wheel drive and four-wheel drive on the fly.
