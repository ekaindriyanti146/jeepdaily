The 2025 Honda Passport TrailSport is the latest iteration of Honda's mid-size SUV, designed to bridge the gap between on-road comfort and off-road capability. With its rugged exterior and advanced all-wheel-drive system, this vehicle is poised to take the off-road world by storm. In this article, we will delve into the history of the Honda Passport, explore its technical details, and examine its real-world performance.

## History/Context

The Honda Passport has a rich history that dates back to the 1990s. Initially, it was a rebadged Isuzu Rodeo, but in 2019, Honda reintroduced the Passport as a mid-size SUV, built on the same platform as the Pilot. The 2025 Honda Passport TrailSport is the latest variant, designed to cater to the growing demand for off-road capable vehicles. With its aggressive exterior design and advanced all-wheel-drive system, the TrailSport is set to appeal to adventure-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts.

The 2025 Honda Passport TrailSport is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine, producing 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission and an advanced all-wheel-drive system, which features a torque vectoring system and a locking rear differential.

The suspension has been tweaked to provide a more comfortable ride on paved roads, while also offering improved articulation and ground clearance for off-road excursions.

The TrailSport also features a unique exterior design, with a more aggressive front fascia, unique wheels, and a distinctive interior trim.

## Real World Performance

The 2025 Honda Passport TrailSport is designed to deliver exceptional performance both on and off the road. With its advanced all-wheel-drive system and locking rear differential, the TrailSport can tackle challenging off-road terrain with ease. The vehicle's ground clearance and approach/departure angles have been optimized to provide a smooth and confident driving experience, even in the most demanding environments. On paved roads, the TrailSport offers a comfortable and refined ride, making it an ideal vehicle for daily driving.

## Comparison/Verdict

The 2025 Honda Passport TrailSport is a formidable competitor in the mid-size SUV segment, offering a unique blend of off-road capability and on-road comfort. Compared to its rivals, such as the Toyota 4Runner and the Ford Edge, the TrailSport stands out with its advanced all-wheel-drive system and aggressive exterior design. While it may not be the most powerful or luxurious vehicle in its class, the TrailSport's versatility and capability make it an attractive option for adventure-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts.
