The Willys MB, also known as the Jeep, has a rich history dating back to World War II. First introduced in 1941, the Willys MB was designed to meet the U.S. Army's need for a lightweight, versatile vehicle that could navigate challenging terrain. Over the years, the Jeep has evolved to become an iconic symbol of off-road adventure and exploration. The 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe Willys ’41 pays homage to this legacy, incorporating classic design elements with modern technology and innovation.
