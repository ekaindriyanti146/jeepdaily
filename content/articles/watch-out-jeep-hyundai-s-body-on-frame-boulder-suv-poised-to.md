The Hyundai Boulder SUV is set to feature a body-on-frame design, which will provide the necessary strength and durability for off-road adventures.

Under the hood, the Boulder SUV is expected to feature a range of engine options, including a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-four and a 3.5-liter V6.

The vehicle will also feature an advanced four-wheel-drive system, complete with a two-speed transfer case and a locking rear differential.

In terms of technology, the Boulder SUV will come equipped with a range of innovative features, including a touchscreen infotainment system, a digital instrument cluster, and a suite of advanced safety features.
