## Section 2: Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension of the Jeep Gladiator

Under the hood, the Jeep Gladiator boasts a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. But what really sets the Gladiator apart is its suspension system. The truck features a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear, providing unparalleled stability and control on even the most treacherous terrain. The Gladiator also features a range of advanced technologies, including a disconnecting front sway bar, which allows for increased articulation and flexibility.
