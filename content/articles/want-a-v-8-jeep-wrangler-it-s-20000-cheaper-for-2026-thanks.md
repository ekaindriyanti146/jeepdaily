## Technical Details of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler V-8

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler V-8 features a 6.4-liter V-8 engine that produces 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and responsive shifting. The Wrangler also features a robust suspension system, which includes a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a track bar in the rear. This setup provides excellent articulation and stability, making the Wrangler well-suited for off-road adventures.

| **Specification** | **Value** |

| Ground Clearance | 10.9 inches |

The Wrangler also features a range of advanced technologies, including a Uconnect infotainment system with an 8.4-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, and a premium audio system with nine speakers.

## Real-World Performance of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler V-8

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler V-8 is a formidable off-road vehicle that is capable of handling even the most challenging terrain. With its powerful V-8 engine and robust suspension system, the Wrangler can tackle steep inclines, rocky trails, and sandy dunes with ease. The vehicle's ground clearance of 10.9 inches and approach angle of 43.9 degrees also make it well-suited for navigating rough terrain.

On the road, the Wrangler V-8 is surprisingly refined and comfortable, with a smooth ride and responsive handling. The vehicle's eight-speed automatic transmission provides seamless shifting, and the V-8 engine delivers plenty of power for merging onto highways and climbing steep grades.
