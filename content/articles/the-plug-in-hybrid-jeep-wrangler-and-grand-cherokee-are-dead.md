In real-world testing, the plug-in hybrid Wrangler and Grand Cherokee models performed admirably, providing improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. However, the plug-in hybrid Wrangler and Grand Cherokee models were not without their drawbacks. Additionally, the high upfront cost of the vehicles made them less competitive with their traditional counterparts.
