## History and Context

The Jeep Gladiator has a rich history dating back to the 1960s, when it was first introduced as a pickup truck version of the iconic CJ-5.

Over the years, the Gladiator has undergone numerous transformations, with each new generation bringing improved performance, capability, and design.

The Convoy Edition is the latest iteration of this legacy, paying homage to the classic Jeep trucks of the past while incorporating modern technology and features.

The two-tone retro look is a nod to the classic Jeep designs of the 1970s and 1980s, with a bold and eye-catching color scheme that is sure to stand out on the trail.
