## Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension of the 2026 Grand Cherokee Trailhawk

The 2026 Jeep Grand Cherokee Trailhawk is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and a two-speed transfer case, allowing for seamless shifting between two-wheel and four-wheel drive.

The Trailhawk trim also features a unique suspension system, which includes a 1-inch lift kit, high-strength steel skid plates, and 18-inch aluminum wheels wrapped in aggressive 31-inch tires.

The vehicle's approach and departure angles have also been improved, making it more capable of tackling steep inclines and declines.
