However, a recent recall has raised concerns among owners and potential buyers. More than 1 million Jeep Wranglers and Gladiators have been recalled due to a potential fire hazard, sparking worries about the safety of these vehicles. In this article, we will delve into the details of the recall, its causes, and the implications for owners.

## Section 1: History/Context

The Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator have a long history of being reliable and safe off-road vehicles. However, this recall is not the first time that Jeep has faced issues with its vehicles. In the past, the company has recalled vehicles due to problems with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The current recall is related to a potential fire hazard caused by an electrical issue. According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), the recall affects 1,025,522 vehicles in the United States, including 2018-2022 Jeep Wranglers and 2020-2022 Jeep Gladiators.
