The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and seamless shifting, whether you're cruising down the highway or navigating treacherous terrain.

The suspension of the Wrangler Moab 392 has also been tweaked to accommodate the increased power output. The vehicle features a uniquely tuned suspension with FOX shocks, allowing for improved articulation and stability.
