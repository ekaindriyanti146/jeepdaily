## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2027 Jeep Wrangler Sarge is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This potent powerplant is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, featuring a two-speed transfer case and a heavy-duty suspension system.

The Sarge boasts a ground clearance of 11.5 inches, with a approach angle of 35.5 degrees and a departure angle of 29.5 degrees.

The vehicle's suspension system features heavy-duty coil springs, gas-filled shock absorbers, and a stabilizer bar, providing a smooth ride and exceptional stability.

The following table highlights the key technical specifications of the 2027 Jeep Wrangler Sarge:

| Feature | Specification |

| Transmission | Eight-speed automatic |

| Ground Clearance | 11.5 inches |
| Approach Angle | 35.5 degrees |
| Departure Angle | 29.5 degrees |
| Curb Weight | 5,100 pounds |
