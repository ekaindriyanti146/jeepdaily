## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Gladiator also features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

The suspension system is designed to provide a smooth ride and excellent handling, both on and off the road.

The Gladiator also features a range of advanced safety features, including electronic stability control, anti-lock brakes, and a range of airbags.
