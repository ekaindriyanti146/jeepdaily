## Introduction to the New Trim
The Jeep Gladiator, known for its rugged design and off-road capabilities, has just introduced a new trim that adds a softer side to the vehicle. This new $47K trim is designed to appeal to a wider range of buyers who want a vehicle that is both capable and comfortable.

### Features of the New Trim
Some of the key features of the new trim include:
* Heated leather seats
* 8.4-inch touchscreen display
* Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration
* Blind-spot monitoring and rear cross-path detection
* 18-inch aluminum wheels

## Design and Interior
The new trim has a more refined design, with a focus on comfort and luxury. The interior features premium materials, including leather seats and metal accents. The exterior design is still rugged and capable, but with a more refined look.
