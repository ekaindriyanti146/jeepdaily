## History and Context

The Jeep Wrangler has a rich history that dates back to World War II, with the Willys MB being one of the first models to bear the Jeep name.

Over the years, Jeep has continued to evolve and improve the Wrangler, incorporating new technologies and design elements while maintaining its core off-road capabilities.

The introduction of the Willys 392 edition signifies a new chapter in this history, where heritage meets modern power and sophistication.

This model is a tribute to the original Willys MB, with its name paying homage to the Willys-Overland Motor Company, which produced the first civilian Jeep models.

The 392 in its name refers to the powerful 6.4-liter HEMI V8 engine that powers this beast, marking a significant departure from the traditional engines used in previous Wrangler models.

The 2026 Wrangler Willys 392 is a marvel of modern engineering, combining the timeless design of the Wrangler with the potent performance of a HEMI engine. Here are some key technical details:

- **Engine**: The heart of the Willys 392 is its 6.4-liter HEMI V8 engine, capable of producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and responsive shifting.

- **Suspension**: The vehicle features a specially tuned suspension system designed to handle the increased power and provide a comfortable ride both on and off the road. This includes a hydraulic shock absorber and a performance-tuned shock calibration.

- **Drivetrain**: The Willys 392 comes with a Selec-Trac full-time four-wheel-drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and heavy-duty axles. This setup allows for maximum traction and control in various driving conditions.
