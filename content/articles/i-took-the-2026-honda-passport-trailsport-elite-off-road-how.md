# Introduction to the 2026 Honda Passport TrailSport Elite

The 2026 Honda Passport TrailSport Elite is the latest iteration of Honda's mid-size SUV, designed to tackle the toughest off-road trails with ease. As a senior automotive journalist, I had the opportunity to take this vehicle off the beaten path and put its capabilities to the test. With its rugged exterior design, advanced all-wheel-drive system, and impressive ground clearance, the Passport TrailSport Elite is poised to take on the likes of the Toyota 4Runner and Ford Bronco.

## History and Context of the Honda Passport

The Honda Passport has a long history dating back to the 1990s, when it was first introduced as a compact SUV. Over the years, the Passport has undergone significant transformations, with each new generation bringing improved capabilities and features. The current generation, introduced in 2019, marked a significant departure from its predecessors, with a more aggressive design and advanced technology features. The TrailSport Elite trim is the latest addition to the Passport lineup, designed specifically for off-road enthusiasts.

## Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension

The 2026 Honda Passport TrailSport Elite is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine, producing 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission and an advanced all-wheel-drive system, which features a torque-vectoring rear differential and a traction management system.

The suspension has been upgraded with stiffer springs and shocks, providing improved stability and control on uneven terrain.

The Passport TrailSport Elite also features a range of off-road-focused technologies, including hill descent control, traction control, and a multi-angle rearview camera.

## Real World Performance of the Passport TrailSport Elite

During my time with the 2026 Honda Passport TrailSport Elite, I had the opportunity to put its off-road capabilities to the test on a variety of terrain, including rocky trails, sandy dunes, and muddy forests. The vehicle performed admirably, with its advanced all-wheel-drive system and torque-vectoring rear differential providing excellent traction and control. The suspension, while stiff, provided a comfortable ride on uneven terrain, and the hill descent control system worked flawlessly on steep inclines.

## Comparison and Verdict

The 2026 Honda Passport TrailSport Elite is a capable off-road vehicle that is well-suited to tackle a variety of terrain. While it may not have the same level of off-road credibility as some of its competitors, such as the Toyota 4Runner or Ford Bronco, it is a solid choice for those looking for a mid-size SUV with advanced off-road capabilities. With its impressive ground clearance, advanced all-wheel-drive system, and range of off-road-focused technologies, the Passport TrailSport Elite is a worthy consideration for anyone looking to venture off the beaten path.
