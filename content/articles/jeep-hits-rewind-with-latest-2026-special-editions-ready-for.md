The concept of special edition vehicles is not new to the automotive industry. Manufacturers have long been creating limited-run models to commemorate significant milestones, celebrate cultural phenomena, or simply to offer customers something unique. In the case of Jeep, the brand has a rich history of producing special edition models that pay homage to its military heritage, outdoor lifestyle, and pop culture icons. The 2026 Special Editions, however, mark a new chapter in this tradition, as they draw inspiration from the iconic 90s arcade era.

This era saw the birth of legendary franchises like Super Mario, Sonic the Hedgehog, and Street Fighter, which have since become ingrained in popular culture. By tapping into this nostalgia, Jeep aims to create a sense of familiarity and shared experience among its customers, while also showcasing its commitment to innovation and creativity.
