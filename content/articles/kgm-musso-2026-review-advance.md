## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood, the 2026 KGM Musso boasts a powerful 2.2L turbo-diesel engine that produces 133kW of power and 400Nm of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting.

The Musso's suspension has also been upgraded, featuring a coil-spring suspension at the front and a five-link live axle at the rear.

This setup provides excellent stability and control, both on and off the road.

Other notable features include a wading depth of up to 789mm, a ground clearance of 215mm, and a maximum towing capacity of 3000kg.

The following table highlights some of the key technical specifications of the 2026 KGM Musso:
| Specification | Detail |
| --- | --- |

| Power | 133kW |
| Torque | 400Nm |
| Transmission | Six-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Coil-spring (front), five-link live axle (rear) |
| Wading Depth | Up to 789mm |
| Ground Clearance | 215mm |
| Towing Capacity | Up to 3000kg |

## Section 3: Real World Performance

So how does the 2026 KGM Musso perform in real-world conditions? To find out, I took this vehicle on a grueling off-road test, tackling everything from steep inclines to muddy bogs.
