## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2016 Jeep Wrangler Unlimited Sport Softtop comes equipped with a 3.6L V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or an optional 5-speed automatic transmission. The vehicle features a robust suspension system, including a heavy-duty Dana 30 front axle and a Dana 35 rear axle, providing exceptional stability and control on both on-road and off-road terrains. The Wrangler Unlimited Sport Softtop also boasts an impressive ground clearance of 8.8 inches, allowing it to tackle challenging trails and obstacles with ease.
