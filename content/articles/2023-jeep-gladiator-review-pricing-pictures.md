The V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the EcoDiesel engine produces 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque.

The Gladiator also features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

The vehicle's advanced four-wheel drive technology includes features such as a two-speed transfer case, a rear locker, and a front anti-roll bar.

The Gladiator's braking system is equally impressive, with 12.9-inch vented rotors in the front and 13.6-inch vented rotors in the rear.
