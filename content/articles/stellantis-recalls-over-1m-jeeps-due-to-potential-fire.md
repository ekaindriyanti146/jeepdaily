## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The technical details of the recall are centered around the faulty fuel pump, which is located inside the fuel tank. The pump is responsible for supplying fuel to the engine, and in this case, it can malfunction and cause a fire. The issue is not limited to a specific engine type or suspension setup, but rather affects various models and years. The affected vehicles include:

| Model | Years Affected | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jeep Cherokee | 2014-2020 | 2.4L and 3.2L engines |
| Jeep Grand Cherokee | 2011-2020 | 3.6L, 5.7L, and 6.4L engines |
| Jeep Wrangler | 2018-2020 | 2.0L and 3.6L engines |

The recall is not related to the suspension or engine performance, but rather a specific component that can fail and cause a fire. Stellantis has stated that they will replace the faulty fuel pump with a new one that meets their safety standards.
