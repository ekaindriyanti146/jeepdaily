## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

At the heart of any reliable truck is its engine and suspension system.

The Gladiator Truck comes equipped with a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, capable of producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic, providing drivers with the flexibility to choose their preferred driving experience.

The truck's suspension system is equally impressive, featuring a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

This setup not only enhances the truck's off-road capabilities but also contributes to a smoother ride on paved roads.

The technical specifications of the Gladiator Truck are as follows:
| Specification | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 3.6-liter V6 |
| Horsepower | 285 hp |
| Torque | 260 lb-ft |
| Transmission | 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Five-link coil suspension (front and rear) |
| Ground Clearance | 11.1 inches |
| Approach/Departure Angles | 43.6/29.2 degrees |
| Towing Capacity | Up to 7,650 pounds |
| Payload Capacity | Up to 1,700 pounds |

## Real World Performance

The real-world performance of the Gladiator Truck is where its reliability truly shines.

Additionally, features such as hill descent control and a forward-facing camera enhance the driver's ability to navigate difficult terrains safely.
