## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 1948 Willys Jeep Pickup featured a number of innovative technical details for its time. The truck's engine was paired with a three-speed manual transmission, which provided smooth shifting and reliable performance. The suspension system consisted of a live front axle with leaf springs, while the rear axle was equipped with a semi-elliptical leaf spring setup. This provided a comfortable ride and stable handling, even when loaded with heavy cargo. The truck's braking system was comprised of drum brakes on all four wheels, which were more than adequate for the vehicle's weight and speed.
