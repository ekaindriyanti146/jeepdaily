## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Gladiator Whitecap is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic transmission. The Whitecap also features a four-wheel-drive system, with a two-speed transfer case and a rear locking differential.

The suspension system consists of a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear. The Whitecap also features a class IV hitch receiver and a heavy-duty engine cooling system.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

The Jeep Gladiator Whitecap is designed to handle both on-road and off-road terrain with ease. The V6 engine provides ample power, while the four-wheel-drive system and locking differential ensure maximum traction. The suspension system is tuned for comfort and stability, making the Whitecap a great choice for daily driving.

Off-road, the Whitecap is capable of handling challenging terrain, including rocks, sand, and mud. The approach and departure angles are 43.6 and 26 degrees, respectively, allowing the Whitecap to navigate steep inclines and declines.
