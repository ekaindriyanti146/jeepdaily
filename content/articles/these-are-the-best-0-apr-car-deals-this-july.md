## FAQ

Q: What is 0% APR financing, and how does it work?
A: 0% APR financing means that you won't be charged any interest on your loan for a specified period of time, typically 36, 48, or 60 months.
Q: What are the benefits of 0% APR financing?
A: The benefits of 0% APR financing include saving money on interest payments, lowering your monthly payments, and getting into a new vehicle with little to no upfront costs.
Q: Are there any drawbacks to 0% APR financing?
A: Yes, one of the main drawbacks is that 0% APR deals often require a high credit score and may have stricter repayment terms.
Q: Can I negotiate the price of the vehicle when taking advantage of a 0% APR deal?
A: Yes, you can still negotiate the price of the vehicle, but be aware that the dealer may have already factored in the cost of the 0% APR financing into the sale price.
