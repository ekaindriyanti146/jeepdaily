## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This potent powerplant is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides seamless shifting and optimal power delivery.

The Moab 392 also features a robust suspension system, with a combination of coil springs and Fox shocks, designed to provide unparalleled stability and control on uneven terrain.

The vehicle's advanced suspension is complemented by a set of 17-inch bead-lock capable wheels, wrapped in 33-inch all-terrain tires, which provide exceptional grip and traction on a variety of surfaces.
