## Section 1: History/Context

The Ford E-250 is a line of vans produced by Ford from 1961 to 2014. The 2001 model year saw significant updates, including a new engine and transmission. The Sportsmobile Agile is a conversion of the E-250, designed to provide a more off-road capable vehicle. The conversion includes a lift kit, larger tires, and a reinforced suspension system. This combination of power and agility makes the 2001 Ford E-250 Sportsmobile Agile Offroad 4x4 a unique and formidable off-road vehicle.

## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2001 Ford E-250 Sportsmobile Agile Offroad 4x4 is powered by a 5.4L V8 engine, producing 255 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission and a two-speed transfer case. The suspension system has been upgraded with a lift kit, providing 4 inches of clearance. The vehicle also features a reinforced axle and larger tires, designed to handle the demands of off-road driving.
