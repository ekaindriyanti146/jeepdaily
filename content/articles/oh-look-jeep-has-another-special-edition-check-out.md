# Introduction to the 2026 Jeep Gladiator Shadow Ops
The Jeep Gladiator has been a staple in the off-road community since its release, and the brand continues to push the boundaries with special edition models. The latest addition to the Gladiator family is the Shadow Ops, a trim level that promises to deliver unparalleled off-road capability and aggressive styling.

## Design and Features
### Exterior Styling
The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Shadow Ops boasts a unique exterior design that sets it apart from other trim levels. Some of the key features include:
* 17-inch black aluminum wheels
* 33-inch all-terrain tires
* Black powder-coated bumpers
* Shadow Ops decals on the hood and rear quarter panels
* Gloss black roof and Freedom Top

### Interior Features
The interior of the Shadow Ops is just as impressive, with features such as:
* Black leather-trimmed seats with Shadow Ops embroidery
* 8.4-inch touchscreen display with Uconnect infotainment system
* Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration
* Premium audio system with 9 speakers

## Capability and Performance
### Off-Road Capability
The Jeep Gladiator Shadow Ops is designed to tackle even the toughest off-road terrain. Some of its key capabilities include:
| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Approach Angle | 43.6 degrees |
| Departure Angle | 26 degrees |
| Breakover Angle | 20.3 degrees |
| Ground Clearance | 11.1 inches |
| Water Fording Depth | Up to 30 inches |

### Engine and Transmission
The Shadow Ops is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission.

## Conclusion
The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Shadow Ops is an impressive addition to the Gladiator family, offering a unique blend of style, capability, and performance. Whether you're tackling the toughest off-road trails or cruising around town, the Shadow Ops is sure to turn heads. With its aggressive styling, advanced features, and unparalleled off-road capability, this special edition model is a must-have for any Jeep enthusiast.
