## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Gladiator is powered by a range of engines, including a 3.6-liter V6 and a 3.0-liter EcoDiesel V6.

The V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the EcoDiesel engine delivers 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque.

Both engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, providing seamless shifting and optimal performance.

The Gladiator also features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

This advanced suspension system provides exceptional stability and control, both on and off the road.
