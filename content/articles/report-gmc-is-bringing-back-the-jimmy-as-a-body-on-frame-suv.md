The Jimmy was available in various trim levels, including the base model, the SLE, and the top-of-the-line SLT.

Over the years, the Jimmy underwent several updates and facelifts, but it remained true to its body-on-frame roots.

The Jimmy was discontinued in 2005, but its legacy lives on, and its revival is expected to generate significant interest among SUV enthusiasts.
