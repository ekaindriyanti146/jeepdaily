## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Wrangler Rewind is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level.

The Rewind model also features a advanced suspension system that includes a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

This suspension system provides a smooth ride on paved roads and can handle the toughest off-road terrain.

The Rewind model also features a range of advanced safety features, including forward collision warning, adaptive cruise control, and blind spot monitoring.

The Jeep Wrangler Rewind is a capable off-road vehicle that can handle a range of terrain, from rocky trails to sandy dunes.
