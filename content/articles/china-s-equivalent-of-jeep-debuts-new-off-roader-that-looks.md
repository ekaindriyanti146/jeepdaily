## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The new off-roader from Great Wall Motors features a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine, producing 210 horsepower and 320 Nm of torque. This engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and a four-wheel-drive system. The vehicle also features a robust suspension system, with coil springs and shock absorbers at all four corners. The suspension travel is impressive, with 220 mm of clearance at the front and 200 mm at the rear. This should provide ample ground clearance for tackling rough terrain.
