## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The recall affects certain model year Jeep Gladiator and Wrangler vehicles, specifically those equipped with a certain type of engine and transmission.

The issue is related to a potential electrical fault that could cause a fire in the vehicle.

The technical details of the recall are as follows:
| Vehicle | Model Year | Engine | Transmission |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jeep Gladiator | 2020-2022 | 3.6L V6 | 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic |
| Jeep Wrangler | 2018-2022 | 3.6L V6 | 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic |
The recall will involve the replacement of certain electrical components and the updating of software to prevent the risk of a fire.

The manufacturer has stated that the repair will be done free of charge and that owners will be notified by mail.
