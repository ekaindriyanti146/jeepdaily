## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2-door Jeep Gladiator boasts an impressive array of technical features, including a maximum payload capacity of up to 1,700 pounds.

The truck is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, which produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic transmission.

The Gladiator's suspension system features a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar, providing a smooth ride and exceptional handling both on- and off-road.

The truck's approach and departure angles are 40.8 degrees and 25.5 degrees, respectively, allowing for impressive maneuverability in challenging terrain.
