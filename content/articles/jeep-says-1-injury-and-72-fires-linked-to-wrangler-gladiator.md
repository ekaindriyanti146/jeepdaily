## History and Context

The Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator recall is a result of a number of reported incidents involving fires and injuries. According to Jeep, there have been 72 reported fires and one injury linked to the recall. The recall affects certain model year 2021 and 2022 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator vehicles, and is related to a faulty fuel pump that can cause a fire. The issue was first identified in 2021, and Jeep has since been working to notify affected owners and provide repairs.

The recall is a significant concern for Jeep, as it has the potential to damage the company's reputation and impact sales. However, it is also an opportunity for Jeep to demonstrate its commitment to safety and customer satisfaction. By taking proactive steps to address the issue and provide repairs, Jeep can help to minimize the impact of the recall and maintain customer trust.

| **Component** | **Description** | **Failure Mode** |
