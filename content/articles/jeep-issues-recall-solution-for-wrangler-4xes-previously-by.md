## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Wrangler 4xe is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine, paired with two electric motors and a 17-kilowatt-hour battery pack. This powertrain produces a combined 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque, allowing the Wrangler 4xe to accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 6 seconds. The vehicle's suspension features a five-link setup in the front and a five-link coil suspension in the rear, providing a smooth ride and excellent articulation. In real-world testing, the Jeep Wrangler 4xe has proven to be a capable and versatile vehicle, both on and off the road. The Jeep Wrangler 4xe is a unique vehicle that combines the best of both worlds: the capability and ruggedness of a traditional Wrangler, with the efficiency and innovative technology of a plug-in hybrid.
