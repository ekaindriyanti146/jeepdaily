The EV9 is expected to come with a range of electric powertrains, offering varying levels of performance and range to suit different needs and preferences.

For the off-road van, Kia has focused on creating a vehicle that is as capable as it is practical.

With advanced all-wheel-drive systems and specialized suspension designed to handle the rigors of off-road driving, this van is poised to tackle challenging terrain with ease.
