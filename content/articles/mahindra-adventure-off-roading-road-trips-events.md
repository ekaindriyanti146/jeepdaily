Mahindra Adventure was launched to cater to the growing demand for off-roading and adventure tourism in India. The program is designed to provide enthusiasts with a platform to explore the country's diverse landscapes, from the rugged Himalayas to the scenic coastal routes. Over the years, Mahindra Adventure has grown in popularity, with a loyal community of participants who embark on these journeys every year. ## Technical Details and Specifications

Mahindra Adventure offers a range of vehicles, including the iconic Thar and the powerful Scorpio, which are designed to tackle the toughest off-roading terrains. The vehicles are equipped with advanced features such as four-wheel drive, high ground clearance, and robust suspension systems. The technical specifications of the vehicles are as follows:

| Vehicle | Engine | Power | Torque | Ground Clearance |

| Scorpio | 2.2L Diesel | 120 BHP | 280 Nm | 180 mm |
