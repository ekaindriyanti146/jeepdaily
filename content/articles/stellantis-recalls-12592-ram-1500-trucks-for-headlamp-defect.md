## Hook

The automotive industry is no stranger to recalls, but when a major manufacturer like Stellantis issues one, it's worth taking notice. Recently, Stellantis announced a recall of approximately 12,592 units of the 2026 Ram 1500 due to a headlamp wiring issue. This defect can cause the parking lamps and daytime running lamps (DRLs) to flicker intermittently or fail to operate altogether, which is a significant safety concern.
