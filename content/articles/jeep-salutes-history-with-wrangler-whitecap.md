Over the years, the brand has evolved, incorporating innovative technologies while maintaining its core values of adventure, freedom, and the pursuit of the great outdoors.

The Wrangler Whitecap is a culmination of this legacy, aiming to appeal to both enthusiasts of vintage Jeep models and newcomers to the brand seeking a unique driving experience.
