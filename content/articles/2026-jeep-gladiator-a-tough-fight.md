The V8 engine, available on the top-of-the-line Rubicon model, produces 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

The Gladiator also features a advanced suspension system, including a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.
