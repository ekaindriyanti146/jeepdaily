The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Sahara 4x4 is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and seamless gear shifts.

The Sahara 4x4 also features a four-wheel-drive system with a two-speed transfer case, allowing drivers to tackle even the most challenging terrain with confidence.

The suspension system has been upgraded to include a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear, providing improved stability and control.

Other notable features include a maximum towing capacity of up to 7,650 pounds and a payload capacity of up to 1,700 pounds.
