## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Rubicon is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and a two-speed transfer case, allowing for seamless shifting between two-wheel and four-wheel drive.

The Rubicon also features a range of suspension upgrades, including heavy-duty axles, electronic locking differentials, and a disconnecting front sway bar.

These enhancements allow for improved articulation and stability, making the Rubicon capable of tackling even the most challenging off-road terrain.

Other notable features include 33-inch all-terrain tires, a rock-traction system, and a range of advanced safety features.
