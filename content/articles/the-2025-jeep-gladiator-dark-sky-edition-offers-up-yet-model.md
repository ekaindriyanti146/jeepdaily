The 2025 Jeep Gladiator Dark Sky Edition is built on the same platform as the standard Gladiator, with a few key enhancements to set it apart.

The vehicle features a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level.

The suspension system has been upgraded with Fox shocks and a disconnecting front sway bar, allowing for improved articulation and control on uneven terrain.

The vehicle also features a unique set of 17-inch wheels with 33-inch tires, providing a more aggressive stance and improved traction.

The 2025 Jeep Gladiator Dark Sky Edition is designed to perform in a variety of off-road environments, from rocky trails to sandy dunes.
