## Section 1: History/Context of the Jurassic Park Jeep

The Jurassic Park Jeep has a rich history that dates back to the original Jurassic Park film released in 1993. The iconic vehicle was used by the park's game warden, Robert Muldoon, and has since become synonymous with off-road adventure and dinosaur-chasing excitement. Over the years, the Jeep has undergone several transformations, with various models being released to commemorate the franchise's anniversaries and new film releases. The latest LEGO version is the largest and most detailed to date, featuring an impressive array of features and accessories.
