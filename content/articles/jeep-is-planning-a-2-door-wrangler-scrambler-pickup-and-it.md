## History and Context

The Jeep Wrangler has a rich history, dating back to the Willys MB, the original military Jeep that saw action in World War II.

Over the years, the Wrangler has evolved, with each generation bringing improvements in design, technology, and capability.

The introduction of the Scrambler pickup is not entirely new, as Jeep has experimented with pickup versions of the Wrangler in the past.

However, the inclusion of an SRT version signifies a commitment to performance, aiming to attract a new demographic of enthusiasts who crave both off-road prowess and on-road exhilaration.

The SRT (Street and Racing Technology) division of Jeep has been instrumental in pushing the boundaries of what is possible with the Wrangler, and their involvement in the Scrambler pickup promises to elevate the model to new heights.
