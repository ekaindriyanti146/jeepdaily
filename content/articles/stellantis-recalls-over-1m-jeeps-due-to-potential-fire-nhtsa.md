## Hook

The automotive industry has seen its fair share of recalls, but the latest one from Stellantis affects over 1 million Jeep vehicles. According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), the recall is due to a potential fire hazard. This is a significant concern for Jeep owners, as it poses a risk to their safety on the road. In this article, we will delve into the details of the recall, the affected models, and what owners can expect.

## Section 1: History/Context

The recall affects various Jeep models, including the Grand Cherokee, Gladiator, and Wrangler. The issue is related to a faulty component that can cause a fire in the vehicle. The NHTSA has issued a statement stating that the recall is a precautionary measure to ensure the safety of Jeep owners. Stellantis has also released a statement, apologizing for the inconvenience and assuring owners that they are working to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. The recall is a reminder of the importance of vehicle safety and the need for manufacturers to prioritize it.

## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The technical details of the recall reveal that the issue is related to a faulty fuel pump relay. The relay can overheat, causing a fire in the vehicle. The affected models include:
| Model | Year | Number of Vehicles Affected |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Grand Cherokee | 2021-2022 | 349,000 |
| Gladiator | 2021-2022 | 131,000 |
| Wrangler | 2021-2022 | 227,000 |
The recall affects a total of over 1 million vehicles. Stellantis will notify owners and repair the vehicles free of charge. The repair involves replacing the faulty fuel pump relay with a new one.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

The real-world performance of the affected Jeep models has been impacted by the recall. Owners have reported issues with their vehicles, including overheating and fires. The recall has also affected the resale value of the vehicles, with some owners experiencing a decrease in value. However, Stellantis has assured owners that the repair will not affect the performance of their vehicles. In fact, the repair will ensure that the vehicles are safe to drive and perform as expected.

## Section 4: Comparison/Verdict

In comparison to other recalls in the automotive industry, the Stellantis recall is significant due to the large number of vehicles affected. However, the company's response to the issue has been swift and proactive. The recall is a reminder that vehicle safety is a top priority, and manufacturers must take all necessary steps to ensure that their vehicles are safe for consumers. In verdict, the Stellantis recall is a significant issue, but the company's response has been adequate. Owners can expect a free repair, and the issue will be resolved as quickly as possible.
