## History/Context

The Jeep Wrangler has a long and storied history that dates back to the 1940s.

Originally designed as a military vehicle, the Wrangler has evolved over the years to become a popular choice for off-road enthusiasts and everyday drivers alike.

With its removable doors, fold-down windshield, and robust 4x4 capabilities, the Wrangler is the ultimate vehicle for exploring the great outdoors.

Over the years, Jeep has continued to update and improve the Wrangler, with significant redesigns in 1997, 2007, and 2018.

As we look to the 2026 model year, it's clear that Jeep is committed to taking the Wrangler to the next level.
