## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Mojave X 4x4 is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and seamless gear shifts. The truck also features a advanced suspension system, including a hydraulic shock absorber and a stabilizer bar, designed to provide a comfortable ride on both on-road and off-road terrain. The Mojave X 4x4 also comes with a range of advanced features, including a terrain management system, hill descent control, and a rear locking differential.
