## Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension

The 2024 Jeep Gladiator boasts an impressive array of technical features, including a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a smooth-shifting eight-speed automatic transmission, allowing for seamless transitions between on-road and off-road driving.

The Gladiator's suspension system has been optimized for improved articulation and stability, featuring a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

The following table highlights the key technical specifications of the 2024 Jeep Gladiator:

| **Specification** | **Description** |

| Horsepower | 285 hp |

| Transmission | Eight-speed automatic |

## Real-World Performance of the 2024 Jeep Gladiator

In real-world testing, the 2024 Jeep Gladiator demonstrated exceptional off-road capabilities, effortlessly navigating challenging terrain and obstacles. The vehicle's advanced four-wheel-drive system, featuring a two-speed transfer case and a 4:1 low-range gear ratio, provided unparalleled traction and control. On-road performance was equally impressive, with the Gladiator exhibiting smooth and responsive handling. The vehicle's advanced technology features, including a high-resolution touchscreen display and a premium audio system, further enhanced the driving experience.
