The M715, a militarized version of the Gladiator, was used by the US military in the 1960s and 1970s. The 2026 Jeep Gladiator Willys '41 pays homage to these vehicles, incorporating design elements and capabilities that made them legendary. With its rugged design, exceptional ground clearance, and four-wheel-drive system, this vehicle is designed to tackle even the toughest off-road terrain.
