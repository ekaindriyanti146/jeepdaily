## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Commando 392 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine that produces a whopping 705 HP and 645 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and a two-speed transfer case, allowing for seamless shifting and optimal power delivery.

The Commando 392 also features a specially designed suspension system, including heavy-duty shocks and springs, to handle the rigors of off-road driving.

The vehicle's chassis has been reinforced with high-strength steel to provide added strength and durability.
