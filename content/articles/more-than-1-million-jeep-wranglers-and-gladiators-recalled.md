## History/Context

The Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator have been staples of the off-road community for decades. Known for their ruggedness and versatility, these vehicles have gained a loyal following. However, with the recent recall, concerns have been raised about the safety of these vehicles. The recall affects certain model years of the Wrangler and Gladiator, with the affected vehicles being produced between 2018 and 2022. The recall is attributed to a potential issue with the vehicle's engine, which can cause a fire risk.

## Real World Performance

In the real world, the recall has had a significant impact on owners of the affected vehicles. Many owners have reported experiencing issues with their vehicles, including engine problems and suspension issues.
