## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Gladiator Shadow Ops boasts an impressive array of technical features that make it a formidable off-road vehicle.

It is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, ensuring that it has the muscle needed to tackle challenging terrains.

The vehicle features a robust suspension system designed to absorb the shocks of off-road driving, providing a smoother ride and better control.

Additionally, the Shadow Ops comes equipped with advanced four-wheel-drive systems, including the Command-Trac 4x4 system, which offers a two-speed transfer case with a low-range gear ratio.

This, combined with its high ground clearance and approach/departure angles, makes the Shadow Ops more than capable of handling demanding off-road conditions.
