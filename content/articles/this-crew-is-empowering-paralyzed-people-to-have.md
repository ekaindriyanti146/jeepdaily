## Introduction to Off-Road Accessibility
The world of off-roading has traditionally been associated with able-bodied individuals, but this crew is changing that narrative. By modifying vehicles and creating specialized equipment, they are making it possible for paralyzed people to participate in off-road adventures.

### Vehicle Modifications for Accessibility
The crew has developed innovative solutions to modify vehicles, ensuring they are safe and accessible for paralyzed drivers. This includes:
* Installing hand controls for acceleration and braking
* Modifying seats for optimal support and comfort
* Adding specialized restraints for secure transportation

#### Technical Specifications of Accessible Vehicles
Some of the key features of these modified vehicles include:
* **Power wheelchair ramps** for easy entry and exit
* **Adaptive seating systems** for optimal support and comfort
* **Hand-controlled acceleration and braking** for safe and controlled driving
