The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting.

The Gladiator also features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

This setup allows for exceptional articulation and stability, making it ideal for off-road adventures.

Other notable features include a maximum towing capacity of up to 7,700 pounds and a payload capacity of up to 1,700 pounds.
