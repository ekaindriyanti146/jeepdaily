## Technical Details of the Jeep Gladiator

So, what makes the Jeep Gladiator tick? Under the hood, the Gladiator boasts a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level. The Gladiator also features a advanced four-wheel-drive system, with a two-speed transfer case and a heavy-duty suspension system. The suspension system includes a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear, providing exceptional articulation and stability.

| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 3.6-liter V6 |
| Horsepower | 285 hp |
| Torque | 260 lb-ft |
| Transmission | 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic |
| Four-wheel-drive system | Two-speed transfer case |
| Suspension | Five-link coil suspension (front), five-link leaf spring suspension (rear) |
| Ground clearance | Up to 11.1 inches |
| Payload capacity | Up to 1,700 pounds |
| Towing capacity | Up to 7,650 pounds |

The Gladiator's technical specifications are a testament to its rugged engineering and capabilities. Whether you're navigating rocky terrain or hauling heavy loads, the Gladiator is designed to handle it all.
