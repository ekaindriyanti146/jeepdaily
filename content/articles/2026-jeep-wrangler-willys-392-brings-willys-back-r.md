When on the trail, the sound of a roaring V-8 engine is music to the ears of any off-road enthusiast. The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Willys 392 is set to bring back the Willys heritage with a powerful 470-HP HEMI V-8 engine. This beast of a vehicle is designed to tackle even the toughest terrain with ease.

## Introduction to the Willys Heritage
The Willys heritage dates back to the 1940s when the Willys MB was first introduced. This iconic vehicle was known for its ruggedness and reliability, making it a favorite among off-road enthusiasts. The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Willys 392 is a tribute to this rich heritage, with a modern twist.

### Design and Features
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Willys 392 features a unique design that pays homage to the original Willys MB. Some of the key features include:
* A distinctive Willys heritage badge
* A black hood decal with a Willys logo
* 18-inch black aluminum wheels with 33-inch tires
* A performance suspension system with FOX shocks
* A Dana 44 heavy-duty axle

#### 3.6L Pentastar V6 Specs
While the 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine is available on other Wrangler models, the Willys 392 comes with a more powerful 6.4L HEMI V-8 engine. Here are some key specs:
| Engine | Horsepower | Torque |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 6.4L HEMI V-8 | 470 HP | 470 lb-ft |

## Off-road Capability
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Willys 392 is designed to tackle even the toughest terrain with ease. Some of the key off-road features include:
* A four-wheel-drive system with a two-speed transfer case
* A locking rear axle
* A disconnecting front sway bar
* 10.9 inches of ground clearance
