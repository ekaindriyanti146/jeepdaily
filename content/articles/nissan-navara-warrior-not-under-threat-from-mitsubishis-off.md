## Section 2: Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension of the Nissan Navara Warrior

The Nissan Navara Warrior is powered by a 2.3-liter twin-turbo diesel engine, producing 140kW of power and 450Nm of torque. This, combined with its advanced suspension system, allows the vehicle to tackle challenging off-road terrains with ease. The suspension system features a coil-spring rear axle and a twin-tube shock absorber, providing a smooth ride and excellent stability. The Navara Warrior also boasts a range of advanced features, including a rear differential lock, hill descent control, and a 360-degree camera system. The technical specifications of the Navara Warrior are as follows:
