## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2024 Mercedes-Benz Sprinter 2500 AWD is powered by a 3.0-liter V6 diesel engine, producing 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting, even in the most demanding off-road conditions. The Sprinter's all-wheel-drive system is equally impressive, with a range of modes designed to tackle different types of terrain. From the standard 2WD mode to the more aggressive 4WD Low mode, the Sprinter's AWD system is designed to provide maximum traction and control, no matter the conditions.

The Sprinter's suspension is equally impressive, with a range of advanced features designed to provide a smooth and comfortable ride, even on the most rugged terrain. The Sprinter's front axle is equipped with a double-wishbone suspension, while the rear axle features a rigid axle with leaf springs. The result is a vehicle that is both capable and comfortable, with a ride that is smooth and refined, even in the most demanding conditions.
