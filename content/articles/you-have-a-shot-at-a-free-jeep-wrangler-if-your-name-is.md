## History and Context of the Jeep Wrangler

The Jeep Wrangler is an iconic off-road vehicle with a rich history dating back to World War II. The first Jeep, the Willys MB, was designed for military use, emphasizing durability, simplicity, and off-road capability. Over the years, the Jeep has evolved, with the Wrangler being the direct descendant of the original Willys MB. Known for its ruggedness, versatility, and unmistakable design, the Jeep Wrangler has become a symbol of adventure and freedom. It's a vehicle that has captured the hearts of many, from seasoned off-roaders to those who simply appreciate its unique style and heritage.
