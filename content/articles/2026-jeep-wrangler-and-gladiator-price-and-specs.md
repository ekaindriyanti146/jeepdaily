The 2026 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator have been updated with new features, including a special anniversary edition to commemorate the brand's rich history. As a seasoned automotive journalist, I've had the chance to get up close and personal with these iconic off-roaders.

## Updates and Changes
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator boast a range of updates, from new exterior colors to enhanced interior features. One of the most notable changes is the introduction of a new infotainment system, which promises to deliver faster processing times and improved connectivity.

### Exterior Updates
The exterior of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator has been refreshed with new colors and wheel designs. The Wrangler is now available in a range of colors, including a new 'High Altitude' finish, while the Gladiator can be ordered in a 'Freedom' edition, which features unique exterior accents and decals.

### Interior Updates
Inside, the 2026 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator have been treated to new materials and technologies. The Wrangler now features a premium leather-trimmed interior, while the Gladiator boasts a new 'Truck' trim level, which adds unique interior accents and features.

## Anniversary Edition
To celebrate the brand's anniversary, Jeep has introduced a special edition Wrangler and Gladiator. The Anniversary Edition features unique exterior and interior accents, including a commemorative badge and exclusive wheel design.

### Features
Some of the key features of the Anniversary Edition include:
* Unique exterior and interior accents
* Commemorative badge
* Exclusive wheel design
* Premium leather-trimmed interior
* Enhanced infotainment system

## Pricing and Specs
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator are priced competitively, with the Wrangler starting at around $30,000 and the Gladiator starting at around $35,000. Here is a breakdown of the specs and prices for each model:

| Model | Price | Engine | Transmission | Drive Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Wrangler Sport | $30,000 | 3.6L V6 | 6-speed manual | 4x4 |
| Wrangler Rubicon | $40,000 | 3.6L V6 | 6-speed manual | 4x4 |
| Gladiator Sport | $35,000 | 3.6L V6 | 6-speed manual | 4x4 |
| Gladiator Rubicon | $45,000 | 3.6L V6 | 6-speed manual | 4x4 |

#### 3.6L V6 Specs
The 3.6L V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, making it well-suited for off-road adventures.

## Off-Road Capability
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator are designed to tackle even the toughest off-road terrain. With features like four-wheel drive, high and low range, and a range of axle ratios, these vehicles are capable of handling anything from rocky hills to muddy trails.

### Features
Some of the key off-road features include:
* Four-wheel drive
* High and low range
* Range of axle ratios
* Skid plates and rock rails
* 33-inch all-terrain tires
