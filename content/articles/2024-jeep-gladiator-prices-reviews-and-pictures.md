The 2024 Jeep Gladiator retains its rugged, boxy design, with a few subtle tweaks to enhance its aesthetic appeal. The vehicle's interior has also been revamped, with premium materials, intuitive controls, and ample cargo space.

#### Key Features:
* **Engine Options**: The 2024 Jeep Gladiator comes with a range of engine options, including a 3.6L V6, a 3.0L EcoDiesel V6, and a 6.4L V8.
* **Transmission**: The vehicle features an 8-speed automatic transmission, with a 6-speed manual transmission available on certain trims.
* **4WD System**: The Jeep Gladiator boasts a robust 4-wheel-drive system, with a 2-speed transfer case and a range of drive modes to tackle various terrain.

### Off-Road Capabilities
The 2024 Jeep Gladiator is designed to conquer even the most challenging off-road terrain. With its high ground clearance, approach and departure angles, and water fording capability, this vehicle can handle anything from rocky trails to sandy dunes.

#### Off-Road Features:
* **Ground Clearance**: The Jeep Gladiator features an impressive 11.1 inches of ground clearance, allowing it to navigate rocky and uneven terrain with ease.
* **Approach and Departure Angles**: The vehicle's approach angle of 43.6 degrees and departure angle of 26 degrees enable it to climb steep inclines and descend steep declines without scraping its underside.
* **Water Fording**: The Jeep Gladiator can ford water up to 30 inches deep, making it an ideal choice for water crossings and swampy terrain.

### Prices and Trims
The 2024 Jeep Gladiator is available in several trims, each with its unique features and price point.

#### Trim Levels:
* **Sport**: The base trim, starting at around $35,000, features a 3.6L V6 engine, 8-speed automatic transmission, and a range of standard features.
* **Sport S**: The mid-level trim, starting at around $38,000, adds features like 17-inch aluminum wheels, a leather-wrapped steering wheel, and a 7-inch touchscreen display.
* **Overland**: The high-end trim, starting at around $42,000, features a 3.0L EcoDiesel V6 engine, 18-inch aluminum wheels, and a range of premium features.
* **Rubicon**: The off-road-focused trim, starting at around $45,000, features a 3.6L V6 engine, 33-inch all-terrain tires, and a range of advanced off-road features.
