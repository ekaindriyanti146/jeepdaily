## Section 3: Real World Performance

In real-world testing, this Chinese 4WD has shown promising results, particularly in off-road scenarios.

Its ground clearance of **220mm** and approach/departure angles of **25°/20°** allow it to navigate challenging terrains with ease.

The vehicle's wading depth of **600mm** is also commendable, making it suitable for water crossings.

On-road, the vehicle provides a comfortable ride, with the suspension doing a good job of absorbing bumps.

However, the engine can feel a bit sluggish at lower revs, a characteristic common to many diesel engines.

Overall, its performance is well-suited for both daily driving and weekend off-road adventures.
