## FAQ

1.

**What is 0% APR financing?**
0% APR financing is a type of loan that allows borrowers to pay no interest on their vehicle purchase for a specified period.
2.

**How long do 0% APR deals typically last?**
0% APR deals can last anywhere from 12 to 60 months, depending on the manufacturer and model.
3.

**Are there any restrictions on 0% APR deals?**
Yes, 0% APR deals often come with restrictions, such as requiring a high credit score or limiting the term of the loan.
