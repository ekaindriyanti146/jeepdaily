## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Kia Tasman is powered by a range of engine options, including a 2.2-liter diesel and a 3.5-liter petrol.

The diesel engine produces 178 horsepower and 313 lb-ft of torque, while the petrol engine produces 276 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque.

The vehicle features a robust suspension system, with a double-wishbone setup at the front and a multi-link setup at the rear.

The Kia Tasman also boasts a range of advanced features, including a terrain-select system, hill descent control, and a rear differential lock.

The vehicle's ground clearance is an impressive 8.4 inches, making it well-suited for navigating rocky and uneven terrain.
