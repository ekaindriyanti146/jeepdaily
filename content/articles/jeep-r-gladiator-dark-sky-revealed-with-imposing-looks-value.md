## Section 2: Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension of the Jeep Gladiator Dark Sky

The Jeep Gladiator Dark Sky is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic transmission. The Dark Sky also features a robust suspension system, including a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear. The vehicle's off-road capabilities are further enhanced by its impressive approach and departure angles, as well as its generous ground clearance.

| **Feature** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 3.6-liter V6 |
| Horsepower | 285 |
| Torque | 260 lb-ft |
| Transmission | Six-speed manual or eight-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Five-link coil (front), five-link leaf spring (rear) |
| Approach Angle | 43.6 degrees |
| Departure Angle | 26.2 degrees |
| Ground Clearance | 11.1 inches |
