The automotive world is abuzz with the latest debut from Jeep - the 2026 Gladiator Whitecap, unveiled as part of the 'Convoy' campaign. ## Section 1: History/Context

The Jeep Gladiator has a rich history, tracing its roots back to the original Willys Jeep trucks of the 1940s.

Over the decades, Jeep has continued to innovate and expand its lineup, with the Gladiator representing a significant milestone in this journey.

Introduced in 2019, the Gladiator quickly gained popularity for its unique blend of Jeep's legendary off-road prowess and the practicality of a pickup truck.

The 'Convoy' campaign, of which the Whitecap is a part, signifies Jeep's commitment to community and adventure, showcasing vehicles that are not just capable but also stylish and connected.

The Whitecap package is designed to enhance the Gladiator's appeal, particularly among those who value a distinctive aesthetic and are drawn to the idea of being part of a convoy - a group of like-minded individuals who share a passion for exploration and the outdoors.
