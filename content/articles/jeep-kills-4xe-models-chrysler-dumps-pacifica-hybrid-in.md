The 4xe lineup included the Wrangler 4xe, Grand Cherokee 4xe, and Renegade 4xe, among others.

These models were designed to provide a more environmentally friendly option for Jeep enthusiasts while maintaining the brand's off-road prowess.

On the other hand, the Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid was a minivan that offered a hybrid powertrain, providing a practical and efficient solution for families.

In real-world testing, the Jeep 4xe models have proven to be capable off-road vehicles, with the electric motor providing instant torque and improved traction.

The Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid, on the other hand, has been praised for its smooth and quiet ride, making it an excellent choice for families.

The decision to discontinue the Jeep 4xe models and the Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid may seem surprising, given the growing demand for electric and hybrid vehicles.
