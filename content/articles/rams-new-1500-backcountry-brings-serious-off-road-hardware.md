The Ram 1500 BackCountry is equipped with an impressive array of off-road hardware, including a robust suspension system, designed to tackle even the toughest terrain.

The vehicle features a combination of coil-link suspension in the rear and an independent suspension in the front, allowing for improved articulation and stability.

The BackCountry also boasts a generous ground clearance of 10.1 inches, enabling it to navigate rocky, muddy, or sandy terrain with ease.

Under the hood, the vehicle is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 305 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque.

For those seeking more power, an optional 5.7-liter V8 engine is available, delivering 395 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque.

| Ground Clearance | 10.1 inches |
| Approach/Departure Angles | 25.2°/26.0° |
