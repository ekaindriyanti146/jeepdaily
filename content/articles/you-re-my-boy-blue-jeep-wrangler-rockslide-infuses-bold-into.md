## Section 2: Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension

The Jeep Wrangler Rockslide is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, although an eight-speed automatic is also available.

The Rockslide also features a number of advanced off-road technologies, including a two-speed transfer case, a locking rear axle, and a disconnecting front sway bar.

The vehicle's suspension system has also been upgraded, with the addition of heavy-duty shocks and springs that provide improved stability and control on uneven terrain.

The Rockslide also features a number of unique design elements, including a bold blue paint color and a set of distinctive 17-inch aluminum wheels.
