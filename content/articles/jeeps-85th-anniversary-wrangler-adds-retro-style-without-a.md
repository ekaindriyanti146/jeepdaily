## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

This engine is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level.

The Wrangler also features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

This suspension system provides a smooth ride on paved roads and excellent articulation on off-road trails.

The Wrangler also features a range of advanced technology features, including a Uconnect infotainment system with a 7-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, and a rearview camera.
