## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

At the heart of the Gladiator Whitecap is a formidable engine, capable of producing the power and torque needed to tackle the toughest winter conditions.

The vehicle comes equipped with a 3.6-liter V6 engine, delivering 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, ensuring that whether you're navigating through snow-covered mountains or icy city streets, you have the power at your disposal.

The engine is paired with a versatile eight-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and efficient shifting, even in the most demanding conditions.

The suspension system, designed to handle the rough and the smooth with equal aplomb, features a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear, ensuring stability, control, and comfort, regardless of the terrain.
