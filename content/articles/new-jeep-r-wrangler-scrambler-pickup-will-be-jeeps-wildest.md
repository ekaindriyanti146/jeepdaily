## History and Context

The Jeep Wrangler has a long and storied history, dating back to the Willys MB of World War II.

Over the years, the Wrangler has evolved to become one of the most iconic and recognizable vehicles on the planet.

The Scrambler nameplate, on the other hand, has its roots in the 1980s, when Jeep introduced the CJ-8 Scrambler, a pickup truck version of the CJ-7.

The new Wrangler Scrambler Pickup is a nod to this heritage, while also incorporating the latest technology and engineering advancements.

With the rise of the pickup truck market, Jeep is poised to capitalize on this trend with a vehicle that combines the best of both worlds.
