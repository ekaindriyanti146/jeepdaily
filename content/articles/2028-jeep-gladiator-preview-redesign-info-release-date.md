The 2028 Jeep Gladiator is set to feature a range of technical upgrades, including a new engine lineup, improved suspension, and advanced technologies.

The 3.6-liter V6 will produce 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.0-liter diesel V6 will produce 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque.

In terms of suspension, the Gladiator will feature a revised coil-link suspension setup, with improved articulation and travel.
