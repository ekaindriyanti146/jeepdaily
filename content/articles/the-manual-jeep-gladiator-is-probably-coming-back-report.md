# The Manual Jeep Gladiator Is Probably Coming Back: Report

The news of the potential return of the manual transmission for the Jeep Gladiator has sparked excitement among fans of the vehicle. After discontinuing the manual gearbox for the 2025 model year, it seems that Jeep may be reconsidering its decision. This move could be a strategic attempt to cater to the demands of enthusiasts who prefer the traditional driving experience offered by a manual transmission.

## Section 1: History/Context

The Jeep Gladiator has a long history of offering manual transmissions, with the first models featuring a three-speed manual gearbox.

Over the years, the automaker has continued to offer manual transmissions in various forms, including the iconic four-speed and five-speed manual gearboxes.

However, with the rise of automatic transmissions, Jeep decided to discontinue the manual option for the 2025 model year.
