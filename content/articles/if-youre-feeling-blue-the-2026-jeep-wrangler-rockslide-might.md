## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Rockslide Special Edition is powered by a 3.6L V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level.

The Rockslide Special Edition also features a range of advanced suspension systems, including a heavy-duty Dana 44 rear axle and a performance-tuned suspension system.

Other notable features include 17-inch black aluminum wheels, 33-inch BFGoodrich All-Terrain T/A KO2 tires, and a range of advanced safety features, such as forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control.
