The 2024 Toyota RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD is the latest iteration of the popular compact SUV, designed to tackle challenging off-road terrain with ease. With its rugged exterior design, advanced all-wheel-drive system, and robust engine, this vehicle is poised to take the off-road world by storm. In this review, we'll delve into the history of the RAV4, explore its technical details, and put it to the test in real-world conditions.

## History and Context

The Toyota RAV4 has been a staple in the compact SUV market since its introduction in 1994.

Over the years, it has undergone several transformations, with each generation building upon the successes of the previous one.

The TRD OFF-ROAD trim, in particular, was introduced to cater to the growing demand for off-road capable vehicles.

The 2024 model builds upon this legacy, with significant upgrades to its suspension, engine, and all-wheel-drive system.

## Technical Details

The 2024 Toyota RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD is powered by a 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine, producing 203 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system, which features a rear differential lock and a multi-mode selector. The vehicle's suspension has been upgraded with TRD-tuned springs and shocks, providing improved stability and control on uneven terrain. Other notable features include:

| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 2.5-liter 4-cylinder |
| Horsepower | 203 hp |
| Torque | 184 lb-ft |
| Transmission | 8-speed automatic |
| All-wheel-drive | Sophisticated system with rear differential lock |
| Suspension | TRD-tuned springs and shocks |
| Ground Clearance | 8.6 inches |
| Approach/Departure Angles | 19/21 degrees |

The RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD also features a range of advanced safety features, including adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and blind spot monitoring.

## Real World Performance

In real-world testing, the 2024 Toyota RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD proved to be a capable and confident off-road performer. The vehicle's advanced all-wheel-drive system and robust engine provided ample traction and power, even in challenging terrain. The TRD-tuned suspension did an excellent job of soaking up bumps and unevenness, providing a smooth and comfortable ride. On paved roads, the RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD was equally impressive, with responsive handling and a quiet, refined cabin.

## Comparison and Verdict

The 2024 Toyota RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD is a strong contender in the compact SUV market, offering a unique blend of off-road capability and on-road refinement. While it may not be as rugged as some of its competitors, such as the Jeep Wrangler, it makes up for it with its advanced features, comfortable interior, and impressive fuel economy. Overall, the RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD is an excellent choice for those seeking a versatile and capable vehicle that can handle both on-road and off-road adventures with ease.

**What is the towing capacity of the 2024 Toyota RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD?**

**Does the RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD come with a locking rear differential?**

**What is the fuel economy of the 2024 Toyota RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD?**

**Is the RAV4 TRD OFF-ROAD available with a manual transmission?**
