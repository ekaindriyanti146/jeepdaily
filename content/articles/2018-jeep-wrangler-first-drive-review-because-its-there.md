## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2018 Jeep Wrangler is available with two engine options: a 3.6L V6 and a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4. The V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the turbocharged engine produces 270 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The Wrangler also features a advanced suspension system, including a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a track bar in the rear. The Wrangler's suspension is designed to provide a smooth ride on pavement while also being capable of handling the toughest off-road terrain.

The Wrangler's interior is also designed to be durable and easy to clean, with features such as waterproof seats and a removable roof.

## Real World Performance

In real-world testing, the 2018 Jeep Wrangler has proven to be a capable and reliable off-road vehicle. Its advanced suspension system and powerful engine options make it well-suited to handling tough terrain, including rocky trails, sandy dunes, and muddy bogs. The Wrangler's interior is also comfortable and well-appointed, with a range of features and options to suit different needs and preferences.

On the road, the Wrangler is also a pleasant surprise, with a smooth ride and responsive handling. The Wrangler's advanced technology features, such as its touchscreen infotainment system and safety features, also make it a great option for daily driving.

## Comparison/Verdict
