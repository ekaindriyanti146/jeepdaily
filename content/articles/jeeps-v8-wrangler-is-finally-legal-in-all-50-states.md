## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The V8 Wrangler boasts an impressive 6.4-liter V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This powerhouse is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and a four-wheel-drive system, making it capable of tackling even the most treacherous terrain.

The Wrangler's suspension has also been upgraded to handle the increased power, with a heavy-duty axle and high-performance shocks.
