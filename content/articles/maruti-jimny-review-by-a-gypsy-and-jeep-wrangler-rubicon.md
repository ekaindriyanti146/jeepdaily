## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Maruti Jimny is powered by a 1.5-liter K15B petrol engine that produces 105 PS of power and 138 Nm of torque.

The engine is mated to a 5-speed manual transmission and features a four-wheel-drive system with a low-range gearbox.

The Jimny's suspension system consists of a coil spring and shock absorber setup at the front, and a rigid axle with coil springs and shock absorbers at the rear.

The vehicle's ground clearance is an impressive 210 mm, which allows it to navigate rocky and uneven terrain with ease.

The Jimny also features a range of safety features, including dual airbags, ABS, and EBD.

## Real World Performance

I had the opportunity to test drive the Maruti Jimny on a range of off-road terrain, including rocky hills, sandy dunes, and muddy trails.

The vehicle performed admirably, with its powerful engine and four-wheel-drive system allowing it to navigate challenging terrain with ease.
