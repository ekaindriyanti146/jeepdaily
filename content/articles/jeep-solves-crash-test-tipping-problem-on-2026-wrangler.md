# Introduction to the 2026 Jeep Wrangler

The Jeep Wrangler is an iconic off-road vehicle that has been a staple of the automotive industry for decades.

Known for its ruggedness and ability to tackle even the toughest terrain, the Wrangler has built a loyal following among off-road enthusiasts.

However, in recent years, the Wrangler has faced criticism for its performance in crash tests, particularly with regards to a tipping problem that has raised concerns about the vehicle's safety.

In response to these concerns, Jeep has made significant improvements to the 2026 Wrangler, addressing the tipping problem and ensuring that the vehicle meets the highest safety standards.

The Jeep Wrangler has a long and storied history that dates back to World War II.

The original Willys MB, the precursor to the modern Wrangler, was designed to be a rugged and reliable vehicle that could withstand the demands of military use.
