The automotive world is abuzz with the latest news from Stellantis, the parent company of Dodge, Chrysler, and Jeep, among others. ## Section 1: History/Context

The history of the American muscle car is deeply intertwined with the story of Dodge.

From the iconic Charger to the formidable Challenger, Dodge has been at the forefront of producing vehicles that embody power, speed, and style.

The V-8 engine, with its distinctive rumble and unparalleled performance, has been the heartbeat of these cars.

Over the years, however, the landscape of the automotive industry has evolved, with a growing emphasis on efficiency, technology, and global competition.

Despite this, the allure of the V-8 muscle car has never truly faded, with many hoping for a revival that would bring back the essence of American automotive prowess.

Stellantis's significant investment in US manufacturing, coupled with the intention to revive the V-8 muscle car, suggests that the company is committed to rekindling this legacy.
