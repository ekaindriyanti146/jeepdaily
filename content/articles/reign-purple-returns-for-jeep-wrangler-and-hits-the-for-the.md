## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator are both known for their off-road prowess, and the Reign Purple color option does nothing to diminish their capabilities.

Under the hood, the Wrangler and Gladiator offer a range of engine options, including a 3.6-liter V6 and a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four.

The Wrangler also offers a 2.2-liter turbocharged diesel engine, while the Gladiator offers a 3.0-liter EcoDiesel V6.

In terms of suspension, both models feature a robust system that is designed to handle the rigors of off-road driving.

The Wrangler features a five-link suspension setup, while the Gladiator features a similar setup with a few key modifications to accommodate its larger size.

The following is a detailed comparison of the two models:
