## Technical Details

The 2027 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator Sarge models come equipped with a range of technical features that make them capable off-road vehicles. Both models feature a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and responsive shifting. The suspension system has been upgraded, with a more robust design that can handle the rigors of off-road driving. The Gladiator Sarge also features a more advanced four-wheel-drive system, with a two-speed transfer case and a rear locking differential.

## Real World Performance

In real-world testing, the 2027 Jeep Wrangler and Gladiator Sarge models have proven themselves to be capable and reliable off-road vehicles. The Wrangler Sarge is agile and responsive, with a tight turning radius and a smooth ride. The Gladiator Sarge, on the other hand, is more suited to heavy-duty off-road use, with its lifted suspension and oversized tires providing ample ground clearance and traction.
