## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler features a range of engine options, including a 3.6-liter V6 and a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four. The V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the turbocharged engine produces 270 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The Wrangler also features a range of suspension options, including a standard coil-spring suspension and an optional adaptive damping system. The interior photos showcase the attention to detail that has gone into the design of the cabin, with premium materials and thoughtful storage solutions throughout.
