# Introduction to Collectible Jeeps
For Jeep enthusiasts, owning a collectible model is a dream come true. With a rich history spanning over eight decades, Jeep has produced some of the most iconic and sought-after vehicles in the world. In this article, we'll explore the top 15 collectible Jeeps that are perfect for any fanatic.

## History of Jeep
Jeep's history dates back to 1941, when the first Willys MB was produced for the US military. Since then, Jeep has become synonymous with off-road capability and adventure. Over the years, Jeep has introduced various models, each with its unique features and characteristics.

### Early Models
Some of the earliest and most collectible Jeep models include:
* Willys MB (1941-1945)
* Willys CJ-2 (1945-1949)
* Willys CJ-3 (1949-1953)
