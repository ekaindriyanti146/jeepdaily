## Section 2: Technical Details

The 2026 Wrangler Moab 392 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine that produces 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and a four-wheel-drive system that features a two-speed transfer case.

The vehicle also boasts a range of advanced off-road technologies, including a disconnecting front sway bar, electronic locking differentials, and a terrain management system with multiple modes.

The Moab 392 also features a unique exterior design that includes a distinctive hood, fender flares, and a set of 17-inch bead-lock capable wheels.

The interior is equally impressive, with premium leather-trimmed seats, a leather-wrapped steering wheel, and a range of advanced infotainment and safety features.
