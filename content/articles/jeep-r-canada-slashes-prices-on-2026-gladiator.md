## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Gladiator boasts an impressive array of technical features, including a powerful 3.6L V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The Gladiator also features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

This setup provides exceptional stability and control, both on and off the road.

The Gladiator's approach and departure angles are also noteworthy, with an approach angle of 43.6 degrees and a departure angle of 26 degrees.
