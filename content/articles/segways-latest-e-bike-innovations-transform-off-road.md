## History and Context of Segway’s E-Bike Development

Segway has been at the forefront of personal transportation innovation for years, and their venture into e-bikes is a natural progression of their mission to provide unique, environmentally friendly solutions for mobility.

The company's history dates back to the early 2000s, with the introduction of the Segway PT, a two-wheeled, self-balancing scooter.

Since then, Segway has expanded its product line to include various models catering to different needs and terrains.

The development of e-bikes marks a significant milestone in Segway's history, combining the company's expertise in balance and navigation technology with the growing demand for electric bicycles.

This move not only expands Segway's market reach but also contributes to the global shift towards more sustainable transportation options.
