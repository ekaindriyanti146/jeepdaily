## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Toyota Tacoma TRD Off Road 4×4 is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 278 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth shifting and optimal power delivery.

The truck features a part-time four-wheel-drive system, which can be switched between two-wheel drive and four-wheel drive modes.

The suspension system consists of a double-wishbone setup in the front and a leaf spring setup in the rear, which provides a smooth ride and excellent off-road capability.

The truck also features a range of advanced technologies, including a multi-terrain select system, crawl control, and a locking rear differential.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

In real-world testing, the 2026 Toyota Tacoma TRD Off Road 4×4 has proven to be a capable and reliable off-road vehicle. The truck's advanced technologies, such as the multi-terrain select system and crawl control, make it easy to navigate challenging terrain, including rocky hills, sandy dunes, and muddy trails.
