## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood, the Gladiator Top Dog boasts a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This potent powerplant is paired with a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission, allowing for seamless transitions between on-road cruising and off-road crawling.

The Top Dog's suspension system is equally impressive, featuring a robust five-link coil suspension setup that provides exceptional articulation and stability.

The truck's approach and departure angles are also noteworthy, with an impressive 40.8-degree approach angle and 25.5-degree departure angle.

## FAQ

1. **What is the towing capacity of the Gladiator Top Dog?**
The Gladiator Top Dog has a maximum towing capacity of up to 7,650 pounds.
2. **Is the Gladiator Top Dog available with a manual transmission?**
No, the Gladiator Top Dog is only available with an 8-speed automatic transmission.
3. **What is the ground clearance of the Gladiator Top Dog?**
