## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Hyundai Boulder Off-Road SUV Concept is built on a sturdy ladder-frame chassis, providing a solid foundation for its off-road exploits. Under the hood, the vehicle is powered by a potent 2.5-liter turbocharged engine, producing an impressive 275 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant is paired with an advanced all-wheel-drive system, featuring a dual-speed transfer case and locking center differential. The suspension setup consists of a heavy-duty coil-spring design, with high-performance shocks and sway bars to enhance stability and control.
