## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The suspension system is equally impressive, with a heavy-duty axle and advanced shock absorbers that provide a smooth ride even on the most rugged terrain.

The vehicle's 4×4 system is also noteworthy, with a two-speed transfer case and electronic locking differentials that provide unparalleled traction and control.
