## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and a two-speed transfer case, allowing for seamless shifting and optimal power delivery. The Moab 392 also features a range of advanced suspension technologies, including a hydraulic shock absorber system and a disconnecting front sway bar. These features work together to provide a smooth and stable ride, both on and off the road.
