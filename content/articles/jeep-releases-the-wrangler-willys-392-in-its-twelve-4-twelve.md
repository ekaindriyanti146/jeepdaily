With the introduction of the Wrangler Willys 392, Jeep is paying homage to its heritage while also pushing the boundaries of what's possible in terms of performance and capability.

The Willys 392 is named after the original Willys MB, which was produced from 1941 to 1945.

The '392' designation refers to the vehicle's powerful 6.4-liter V8 engine, which produces 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.
