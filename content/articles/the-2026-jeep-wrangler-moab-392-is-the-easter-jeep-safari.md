The Moab 392 is the latest iteration of this legacy, named after the infamous Moab off-road trail in Utah, which is notorious for its challenging terrain and breathtaking scenery.

## Technical Details

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler Moab 392 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine, producing 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This beast of an engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides seamless shifting and optimal power delivery.

The Moab 392 features a robust suspension system, with a lifted design that provides 12.9 inches of ground clearance, making it capable of tackling even the most challenging off-road terrain.

The vehicle also features 35-inch all-terrain tires, which provide exceptional grip and traction on a variety of surfaces.

The Moab 392 boasts an impressive approach angle of 51.7 degrees and a departure angle of 43.4 degrees, making it capable of navigating steep inclines and declines with ease.
