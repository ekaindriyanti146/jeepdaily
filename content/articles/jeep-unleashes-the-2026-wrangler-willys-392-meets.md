### Heritage of the Willys Brand
The Willys brand has a long and storied history, dating back to the early 20th century. The company's first vehicle, the Willys Overland, was introduced in 1908 and quickly gained a reputation for its durability and off-road capability. The Willys brand continued to innovate and push the boundaries of what was possible, introducing the first civilian Jeep, the CJ-2, in 1945.
