## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Recon EV features a dual-electric motor powertrain, which produces an impressive 650 horsepower. This powertrain is paired with a high-capacity battery pack, which provides an estimated range of over 300 miles on a single charge. The vehicle also features a advanced all-wheel-drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and a locking rear differential.

The suspension system on the Jeep Recon EV is designed to provide a smooth ride on the road, while also being capable of handling the rigors of off-road driving. The vehicle features a independent front suspension, with a multi-link rear suspension. This setup provides excellent articulation and stability, both on and off the road.
