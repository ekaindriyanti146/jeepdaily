## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Gladiator Rubicon with the Shadow Ops Package boasts a range of technical specifications that make it an off-road powerhouse.

At its heart is a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic.

The suspension system features heavy-duty Dana 44 front and rear axles, with an electronic locking rear axle and an optional front axle locker, ensuring maximum traction and control in various terrains.

The addition of a factory winch, part of the Shadow Ops Package, is a significant enhancement, providing a convenient and reliable means of self-recovery or assisting others in need.

The winch is complemented by other features such as steel rock rails, a front off-road camera, and 17-inch black wheels with 33-inch tires, all contributing to the vehicle's off-road prowess.
