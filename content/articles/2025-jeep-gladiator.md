## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2025 Jeep Gladiator is available with a range of engine options, including a 3.6-liter V6 and a 3.0-liter diesel V6.

The V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the diesel engine produces 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque.

Both engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, and feature a range of advanced technologies, including engine stop-start and adaptive cruise control.

The Gladiator also features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

This setup provides excellent articulation and control, both on and off the road.

The following table summarizes the key technical details of the 2025 Jeep Gladiator:
| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 3.6-liter V6 or 3.0-liter diesel V6 |
| Transmission | Eight-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Five-link coil suspension (front), five-link leaf spring suspension (rear) |
| Ground Clearance | 11.1 inches |
| Approach Angle | 43.6 degrees |

| Breakover Angle | 20.3 degrees |
| Towing Capacity | Up to 7,650 pounds |
| Payload Capacity | Up to 1,700 pounds |
