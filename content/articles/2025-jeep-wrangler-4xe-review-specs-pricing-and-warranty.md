The introduction of the 4xe plug-in hybrid variant marks a significant milestone in the Wrangler's evolution, as it combines the traditional off-road prowess with modern eco-friendly technology.

The 2025 model year brings further refinements, including updated styling, improved infotainment systems, and enhanced safety features.

## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine, paired with an electric motor and a 17-kilowatt-hour lithium-ion battery pack.

This combination produces a total system output of 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

The vehicle features a TorqueFlite eight-speed automatic transmission and a Selec-Trac four-wheel-drive system.

The suspension consists of a heavy-duty Dana 44 front axle and a Dana 35 rear axle, with gas-filled shock absorbers and stabilizer bars.

The Wrangler 4xe also features a unique regenerative braking system, which captures kinetic energy and converts it into electrical energy to recharge the battery.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

The 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe delivers exceptional real-world performance, with impressive acceleration, smooth power delivery, and enhanced off-road capability. The electric motor provides instant torque, allowing for quick and effortless acceleration from a standstill. The vehicle's advanced four-wheel-drive system and heavy-duty axles enable it to tackle challenging terrain with ease, including rocky inclines, sandy dunes, and muddy trails. In electric mode, the Wrangler 4xe can reach speeds of up to 75 mph, making it an ideal choice for daily commutes and urban driving.

## Section 4: Comparison/Verdict

The 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe is a unique offering in the off-road market, with its plug-in hybrid powertrain and advanced technology features.

Compared to its competitors, such as the Toyota 4Runner and Ford Bronco, the Wrangler 4xe offers a distinct blend of power, efficiency, and capability.

While it may not be the most affordable option, its premium features, luxurious interior, and unparalleled off-road performance make it a compelling choice for enthusiasts and adventure-seekers.

With a starting price of around $51,000 and a comprehensive warranty package, the 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe is an attractive option for those seeking a revolutionary off-road experience.

### FAQ

1.

**What is the electric range of the 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe?**
The 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe has an electric range of up to 25 miles, depending on driving conditions and terrain.
2.

**Can the Wrangler 4xe be charged using a household outlet?**
Yes, the Wrangler 4xe can be charged using a standard 120-volt household outlet, as well as a 240-volt Level 2 charger.
3.

**Is the Wrangler 4xe available with a manual transmission?**
No, the 2025 Jeep Wrangler 4xe is only available with an eight-speed automatic transmission.
4.

**What is the warranty coverage for the Wrangler 4xe's battery pack?**
The Wrangler 4xe's battery pack is covered by an 8-year/100,000-mile warranty, providing owners with peace of mind and protection against battery degradation.
