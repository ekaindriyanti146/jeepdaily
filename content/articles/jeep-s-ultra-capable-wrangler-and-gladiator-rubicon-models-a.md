## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

So, what makes the Wrangler and Gladiator Rubicon models so capable? Let's take a closer look at their technical specifications.

Both vehicles come equipped with a powerful 3.6L V6 engine, which produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission, which provides seamless power delivery and optimal gearing for both on- and off-road driving.

The Rubicon models also feature a range of advanced suspension systems, including a disconnecting front sway bar and a set of high-performance shocks, which allow for increased articulation and stability on uneven terrain.

Other notable features include a set of 33-inch all-terrain tires, a heavy-duty axle, and a range of advanced traction control systems.
