## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Gladiator is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, which provides smooth and seamless shifting. The Gladiator also features a robust suspension system, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear. The suspension is designed to provide a smooth ride on the road, while also being capable of handling the toughest off-road terrain. The Gladiator's specs are as follows:
