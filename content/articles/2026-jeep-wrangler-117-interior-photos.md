## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler comes with a range of engine options, including a 3.6-liter V6, a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four, and a 3.0-liter EcoDiesel V6.

Each engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, although a six-speed manual is available on certain trims.

The Wrangler's suspension is designed to handle the toughest off-road terrain, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

The vehicle also features a range of advanced technology features, including a touchscreen infotainment system, navigation, and a suite of safety features like forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control.

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler is a capable off-road vehicle that can handle a range of terrain, from rocky trails to sandy dunes.
