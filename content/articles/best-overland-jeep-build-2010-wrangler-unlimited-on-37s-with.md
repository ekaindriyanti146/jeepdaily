## Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2010 Wrangler Unlimited is powered by a 3.8-liter V6 engine, producing 202 horsepower and 237 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, allowing for precise control and optimal power delivery.

The suspension system features a five-link coil setup in the front and a leaf spring design in the rear, providing a smooth ride and exceptional articulation.

The vehicle is equipped with 37-inch tires, which significantly enhance its ground clearance and traction capabilities.

The stretched M416 trailer is designed to match the Wrangler's off-road prowess, featuring a reinforced frame, heavy-duty axles, and oversized tires.

The trailer is also equipped with a custom storage system, allowing for efficient organization and access to gear.
