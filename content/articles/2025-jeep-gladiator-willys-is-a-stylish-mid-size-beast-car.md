## Technical Details of the 2025 Jeep Gladiator Willys

The 2025 Jeep Gladiator Willys is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission, which provides smooth and precise shifting.

The truck also features a four-wheel drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and a rear axle with a 3.73 ratio.

The suspension system consists of a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

The truck also features 17-inch aluminum wheels with 32-inch all-terrain tires, which provide excellent traction and ground clearance.
