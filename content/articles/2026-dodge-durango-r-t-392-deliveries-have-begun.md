## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Dodge Durango R/T 392 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 HEMI engine that produces 475 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and quick gear shifts.

The Durango R/T 392 also features a performance-tuned suspension, which includes stiffer springs and shocks, designed to improve handling and stability, especially during high-performance driving.

Additionally, it comes equipped with 20-inch aluminum wheels and Pirelli 295/45ZR20 all-season performance tires, which provide the necessary grip and traction to handle the power of the HEMI engine.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

In real-world driving conditions, the 2026 Dodge Durango R/T 392 delivers on its performance promises.
