## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2025 Jeep Wrangler Rubicon 392 Final Edition is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine that produces 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and a heavy-duty four-wheel-drive system, featuring a two-speed transfer case and electronic locking differentials. The suspension system has been upgraded with heavy-duty shocks, springs, and stabilizer bars, providing improved stability and control on both on-road and off-road terrain. The vehicle also features a unique exhaust system, with dual exhaust pipes and a performance-tuned muffler.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

The 2025 Jeep Wrangler Rubicon 392 Final Edition is a beast on the road, with its powerful V8 engine and smooth-shifting transmission providing effortless acceleration and responsive handling.
