Jeep has been working towards electrifying its lineup, with plans to offer a range of hybrid and electric vehicles in the coming years. However, the company has recently announced that it is canceling its plan for a plug-in hybrid version of the Gladiator pickup truck.

The Gladiator has been a successful model for Jeep, and the company had planned to offer a plug-in hybrid version to appeal to customers who are looking for a more environmentally friendly option.
