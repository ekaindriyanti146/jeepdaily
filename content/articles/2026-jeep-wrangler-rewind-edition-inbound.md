## Section 1: History/Context

The Jeep Wrangler has a long and storied history that dates back to the 1940s.

Originally designed as a military vehicle, the Wrangler has evolved over the years to become a popular choice for off-road enthusiasts.

The Rewind Edition is a nod to the Wrangler's heritage, with a design that recalls the classic CJ-5 and CJ-7 models of the 1970s and 1980s.

With its retro-inspired styling, the Rewind Edition is sure to appeal to fans of the original Wrangler.

## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood, the 2026 Jeep Wrangler Rewind Edition is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic. The Rewind Edition also features a range of suspension upgrades, including heavy-duty shocks and springs, that help to improve its off-road capability. Other notable features include a limited-slip rear differential, 33-inch all-terrain tires, and a range of advanced safety features like forward collision warning and blind-spot monitoring.
