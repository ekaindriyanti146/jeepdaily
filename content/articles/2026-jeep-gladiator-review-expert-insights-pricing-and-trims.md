## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Gladiator comes with a range of engine options, including a 3.6-liter V6 and a 3.0-liter V6 diesel.

The V6 engine produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the diesel engine produces 260 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque.

The Gladiator also features a range of transmission options, including a six-speed manual and an eight-speed automatic.

The suspension system is equally impressive, with a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

The Gladiator also features a range of advanced technology features, including a Uconnect infotainment system with a 7-inch touchscreen display and a range of safety features, including forward collision warning and blind spot monitoring.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

In real-world testing, the 2026 Jeep Gladiator has proven to be a formidable performer. The V6 engine provides plenty of power and torque, making it easy to accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 7.5 seconds. The diesel engine is equally impressive, with its massive torque output making it ideal for towing and hauling heavy loads. The Gladiator's suspension system is also noteworthy, providing a smooth and comfortable ride both on and off the road.
