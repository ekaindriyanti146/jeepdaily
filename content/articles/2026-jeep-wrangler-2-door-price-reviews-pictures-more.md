## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2026 Jeep Wrangler 2 Door is equipped with a powerful 3.6L V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This potent powerplant is paired with a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission, allowing for seamless transitions between gears.

The Wrangler's suspension system is equally impressive, featuring a robust five-link coil suspension in the front and a trailing arm coil suspension in the rear.

This setup provides unparalleled stability and control, both on and off the road.

The following table highlights the key technical specifications of the 2026 Jeep Wrangler 2 Door:
| Specification | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 3.6L V6 |
| Horsepower | 285 |
| Torque | 260 lb-ft |
| Transmission | 8-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Five-link coil (front), trailing arm coil (rear) |
| Ground Clearance | 10.8 inches |
| Approach Angle | 34.6 degrees |
| Departure Angle | 29.2 degrees |
| Breakover Angle | 20.8 degrees |
The 2026 Jeep Wrangler 2 Door's technical specifications are a testament to its off-road prowess, with a winning combination of power, suspension, and capability.
