## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2025 Jeep Gladiator Dark Sky Edition is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and seamless shifting.

The suspension system features a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar, providing exceptional stability and control.

The Dark Sky Edition also features a range of innovative technologies, including a advanced four-wheel-drive system, electronic locking rear differential, and a disconnecting front sway bar.

The vehicle's aggressive stance is courtesy of its 33-inch all-terrain tires and 17-inch black aluminum wheels.
