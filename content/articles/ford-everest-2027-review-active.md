The Ford Everest has a rich history that dates back to 2003 when it was first introduced as a mid-size SUV.

Over the years, it has undergone several transformations, each time emerging stronger, more capable, and more technologically advanced.

The 2027 model, particularly the Active trim, represents the pinnacle of this evolution, incorporating feedback from off-road enthusiasts, advancements in automotive technology, and a deep understanding of what makes an off-road vehicle truly great.

The Active trim is specifically designed for those who don't just want to own an SUV but want to experience the thrill of off-roading without compromising on comfort and safety.

### Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

Under the hood, the 2027 Ford Everest Active packs a potent 3.0-liter V6 diesel engine, capable of producing 184 kW of power and 600 Nm of torque.

### Frequently Asked Questions

1. **What is the towing capacity of the 2027 Ford Everest Active?**

- The towing capacity of the 2027 Ford Everest Active can vary depending on the specific configuration and the type of trailer being towed, but it is capable of towing up to 3100 kg.

2. **Does the Ford Everest Active come with a manual transmission option?**

- No, the 2027 Ford Everest Active is available with a 10-speed automatic transmission only.

3. **Is the Ford Everest Active available in different trim levels?**

- Yes, the Ford Everest is available in several trim levels, with the Active being one of the models specifically designed for off-road enthusiasts.

4. **What advanced safety features does the 2027 Ford Everest Active offer?**
