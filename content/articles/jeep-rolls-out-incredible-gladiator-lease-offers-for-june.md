The truck also features a advanced suspension system, including a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension with a stabilizer bar in the rear.

This system provides a smooth ride on the road while also allowing for impressive articulation and control off the road.

The Gladiator also features a range of advanced technology features, including an 8.4-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration, and a premium audio system.
