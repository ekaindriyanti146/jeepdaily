## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

This includes knowing the capabilities of your engine, the suspension system, and how different gear ratios can affect your vehicle's performance.

For instance, a vehicle with a robust engine and appropriately tuned suspension can handle steep inclines and rocky paths with ease.

Moreover, the choice of tires and the vehicle's ground clearance play significant roles in determining its off-road prowess.

Trails Offroad not only provides information on these technical details but also offers insights into how to modify your vehicle for improved off-road performance.
