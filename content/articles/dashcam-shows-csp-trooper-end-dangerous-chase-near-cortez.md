## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The PIT maneuver relies on the principles of physics and vehicle dynamics.

By applying a controlled amount of force to the vehicle, the officer can disrupt its kinetic energy and cause it to spin out of control.

The technique involves the officer positioning their vehicle at a 20- to 30-degree angle to the fleeing vehicle, with the front bumper aligned with the rear quarter panel of the suspect's vehicle.

The officer then applies a gentle to moderate amount of force to the suspect's vehicle, using their own vehicle's momentum to transfer energy and initiate the spin.
