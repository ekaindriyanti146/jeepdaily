## The Origins of the Jeep Name
The term 'Jeep' is believed to have originated from the military term 'GP,' which stands for 'General Purpose.' Over time, the term 'GP' evolved into 'Jeep,' and the name stuck. However, there is another theory that suggests the name 'Jeep' came from a character in the popular comic strip 'Popeye,' known as 'Eugene the Jeep.' This character was a small, magical creature that could move quickly and quietly, much like the vehicle that would eventually bear its name.
