#### FAQs

1. **What are the primary risks associated with off-roading?**

- The primary risks include rollovers, collisions with obstacles, and getting stuck in challenging terrain.

2. **How can off-roaders minimize their risk of accident?**

- By ensuring their vehicle is properly maintained, understanding and using safety features, and never underestimating the power of nature and the limitations of their vehicle and driving skills.

3. **What role do influencers play in promoting off-road safety?**

- Influencers can significantly impact attitudes towards safety by promoting responsible off-roading practices, sharing safety tips, and highlighting the importance of respecting the environment and one's own limits.

4. **Are there any specific safety gear or modifications that are considered essential for off-roading?**

- Yes, items such as a winch, recovery straps, first aid kits, and communication devices are considered essential. Vehicle modifications should be done with safety and performance in mind, ensuring they do not compromise the vehicle's stability or maneuverability.
