## History and Context of the TFL Off-Road Index Leaderboard

The TFL Off-Road Index Leaderboard is the brainchild of TFLcar, a renowned automotive review website. The ORI was created to provide a standardized framework for evaluating off-road vehicles, taking into account factors such as ground clearance, approach and departure angles, and suspension travel. The leaderboard is updated regularly to reflect the latest models and technological advancements in the off-road segment. With the ORI, enthusiasts and prospective buyers can easily compare and contrast different vehicles, making informed decisions about their next off-road adventure.
