## Technical Details of the 2026 Ram 2500 Warlock

The 2026 Ram 2500 Warlock boasts an impressive array of technical features that set it apart from its competitors.

At its heart is a powerful 6.7-liter Cummins Turbo Diesel engine, capable of producing 400 horsepower and a whopping 1,000 lb-ft of torque.

This massive power plant is paired with a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission, ensuring that power is delivered to the wheels exactly when it's needed.

The suspension system has been specifically tuned for off-road performance, with a combination of coil-link suspension in the front and a five-link coil suspension in the rear, providing both comfort and capability.

| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Engine | 6.7-liter Cummins Turbo Diesel |
| Horsepower | 400 hp |
| Torque | 1,000 lb-ft |
| Transmission | 8-speed automatic |
| Suspension | Coil-link front, five-link coil rear |
| Drive Type | Four-wheel drive |
| Ground Clearance | 11.1 inches |
| Approach/Departure Angles | 33.9°/26.2° |

The Warlock also comes equipped with a range of off-road specific features, including hill descent control, an electronic rear locker, and a disconnecting front sway bar. These features, combined with its impressive ground clearance and approach/departure angles, make the Warlock more than capable of handling even the most challenging off-road terrains.
