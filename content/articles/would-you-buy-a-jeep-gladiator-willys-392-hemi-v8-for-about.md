## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Gladiator Willys 392 HEMI V8 is powered by a 6.4-liter V8 engine that produces 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and a four-wheel-drive system, which includes a two-speed transfer case and a rear locking differential. The truck also features a heavy-duty suspension system, which includes a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear. The Willys 392 also comes with 33-inch all-terrain tires, which provide excellent traction and stability on both on-road and off-road terrain.

## Section 3: Real World Performance

In real-world testing, the Jeep Gladiator Willys 392 HEMI V8 has proven to be a capable and powerful truck.
