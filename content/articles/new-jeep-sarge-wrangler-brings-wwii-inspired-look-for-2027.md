## Section 2: Technical Details, Engine, and Suspension

Under the hood, the 2027 'Sarge' Wrangler packs a potent 3.6L V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission, which provides seamless power delivery and exceptional low-end torque. The 'Sarge' Wrangler also features a robust 4-wheel drive system, with a 2-speed transfer case and a set of heavy-duty axles.

The suspension system is equally impressive, with a set of heavy-duty coil springs and gas-filled shock absorbers that provide a smooth ride and exceptional articulation. The 'Sarge' Wrangler also features a range of advanced off-road technologies, including hill descent control, rock mode, and a set of skid plates to protect the underside of the vehicle.
