## History and Context of the Jeep Scrambler

The Jeep Scrambler is not a new concept, but rather a revival of a classic. The original Scrambler was introduced in the 1980s as a CJ-8, a long-wheelbase version of the iconic CJ-5. The new Scrambler, however, is built on the same platform as the Gladiator, with a few key differences. The most notable of these is the addition of a Hemi V8 engine, which provides unparalleled power and torque. With a rich history and a reputation for excellence, the Jeep Scrambler is poised to take the off-road world by storm.
