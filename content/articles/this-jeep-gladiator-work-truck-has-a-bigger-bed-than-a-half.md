## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The Jeep Gladiator Work Truck is equipped with a powerful 3.6-liter V6 engine, producing 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission, depending on the trim level.

The truck features a robust suspension system, including a five-link coil suspension in the front and a five-link leaf spring suspension in the rear.

This setup provides a smooth ride and exceptional handling, both on and off the road.

The Gladiator Work Truck also boasts a maximum towing capacity of up to 7,650 pounds and a payload capacity of up to 1,700 pounds, making it an ideal choice for heavy-duty applications.
