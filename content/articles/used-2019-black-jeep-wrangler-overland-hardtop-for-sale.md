## Section 2: Technical Details/Engine/Suspension

The 2019 Black Jeep Wrangler Overland Hardtop is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque.

The Wrangler's suspension system is equally impressive, with a five-link coil suspension setup that provides excellent articulation and stability on uneven terrain.

The vehicle also features a robust four-wheel-drive system that includes a two-speed transfer case and a rear axle with a 3.45:1 ratio.
