## Frequently Asked Questions
#### Q: What is the death wobble, and how can I prevent it in my Jeep Gladiator 4xe?
A: The death wobble is a phenomenon that occurs when a vehicle's wheels begin to oscillate wildly, causing a loss of control. To prevent the death wobble in your Gladiator 4xe, make sure to maintain proper tire pressure and alignment, and avoid driving at high speeds on rough terrain.

#### Q: What are lockers, and how do they improve off-road performance?
A: Lockers are devices that allow drivers to lock the axles of their vehicle, providing maximum traction and control. Lockers can be especially useful in low-speed off-road situations, such as rock crawling or mud bogging.

#### Q: What are lift kits, and how can they improve my Jeep Gladiator 4xe's off-road capability?
A: Lift kits are modifications that raise the suspension of a vehicle, providing increased ground clearance and improved approach and departure angles. Lift kits can be a useful upgrade for off-road enthusiasts, but they should be installed by a qualified mechanic to ensure proper fitment and safety.
